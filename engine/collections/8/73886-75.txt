Under the Köppen climate classification , Quito has a subtropical highland climate ( Cfb ) . Because of its elevation and proximity to the equator , Quito has a fairly constant cool climate . The average temperature at noon is 18.7 ° C ( 65.7 ° F ) with a normal night - time low of 9.3 ° C ( 48.7 ° F ) . The annual average temperature is 14 ° C ( 57 ° F ) . The city has only two seasons : dry and wet . The dry season , June through September ( 4 months ) , is referred to as summer ; the wet season , October through May ( 8 months ) , is referred to as winter . Annual precipitation , depending on location , is about 1,000 mm ( 39 in ) .
