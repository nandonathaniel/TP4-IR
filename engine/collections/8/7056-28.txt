Since the 1920s , Coca - Cola has been a publicly traded company . One share of stock purchased in 1919 for $40 , with all dividends reinvested , would be worth $9.8 million in 2012 , a 10.7 % annual increase , adjusted for inflation . In 1987 , Coca - Cola once again became one of the 30 stocks which makes up the Dow Jones Industrial Average , which is commonly referenced as a proxy for stock market performance ; it had previously been a Dow stock from 1932 to 1935 . Coca - Cola has paid a dividend , increasing each year for 49 years . Stock is available from a direct purchase program , through Computershare Trust Company , but unlike many programs , has investment fees .
