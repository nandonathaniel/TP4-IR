Working is permitted from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. , except that the minor may be employed until 9 p.m. during the periods outside the school year ( June 1 through Labor Day , depending on local standards ) . Maximum 3 hours on a school day . Maximum 18 hours in a school week . Maximum 8 hours on a non-school day . Maximum 40 hours in a non-school week .
