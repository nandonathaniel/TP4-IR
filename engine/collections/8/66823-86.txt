Canadian singer Connie Kaldor recorded the song on the Gordon Lightfoot tribute album Beautiful . A Tribute to Gordon Lightfoot in 2003 . Canadian musician Neil Young released a cover of this song on his 2014 album A Letter Home . Canadian jazz singer Diana Krall released a cover as a duet with Sarah McLachlan on `` The Complete Sessions '' release of Krall 's 2015 album Wallflower .
