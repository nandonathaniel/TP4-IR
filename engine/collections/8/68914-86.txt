Edmond Hayes ( Callard Harris ) is Cameron Hayes ' son and business partner . He is from South Armagh . Throughout Season 2 , he has as much clout in the business as his father , often making deals and judgment calls on his own . Edmond becomes a rat and is later killed by agent Stahl when he starts to run , after punching her in abdomen when she says his , `` da raised an Irish p * ssy '' . Edmond 's death is framed on Gamma , by agent Stahl , after she follows Polly inside , and kills her in self - defense , just after Stahl kills Edmond .
