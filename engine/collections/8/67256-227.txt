In May 2005 , a NASA press release said that the consensus was that Voyager 1 was then in the heliosheath . In a scientific session at the American Geophysical Union meeting in New Orleans on the morning of May 25 , 2005 , Dr. Ed Stone presented evidence that the craft crossed the termination shock in late 2004 . This event is estimated to have occurred on December 15 , 2004 at a distance of 94 AU from the Sun .
