Prohibited : These weapons generally can not be possessed by civilians , and include fully automatic weapons and many military arms , and handguns with barrel length equal to or shorter than 4.1 inches ( 105 mm ) , and those chambered for . 25 and . 32 cartridges . Normally , the only way to possess these is by being grandfathered in or through inheritance . Most magazines for semi-automatic long guns capable of holding more than 5 centerfire cartridges or 10 rounds for handguns are prohibited .
