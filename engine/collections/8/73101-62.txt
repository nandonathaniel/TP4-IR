The 2013 season saw the resurgence of foundation clubs the Sydney Roosters and the South Sydney Rabbitohs with both clubs finishing first and second place respectively . The NRL was also left with a black eye after the Cronulla Sharks were caught using performance - enhancing substances by ASADA dating back to 2011 . The 2013 Grand Final was played between the Sydney Roosters and the Manly Sea Eagles with the Roosters recording a 26 - 18 victory , the match is most remembered for a spectacular try by Roosters centre Michael Jennings which sealed the win for the Bondi club .
