If 4 -- 5 Iyar occur on a Sunday - Monday , the observances are delayed to Monday - Tuesday , 5 -- 6 Iyar . If 4 -- 5 Iyar occur on a Tuesday - Wednesday , the observances are not moved . If 4 -- 5 Iyar occur on a Thursday - Friday , the observances are advanced to Wednesday - Thursday , 3 -- 4 Iyar . If 4 -- 5 Iyar occur on a Friday - Shabbat , the observances are advanced to Wednesday - Thursday , 2 -- 3 Iyar .
