Hippie Tree -- A tree with sunglasses and dreadlocks who often speaks in hippie slang . Madame Willow ( voiced by Joan Gerber ) -- Also known as the Dowager Tree , Madame Willow is an older , elitist female tree with a lorgnette . Chief Redwood ( voiced by Walker Edmiston ) -- Also known as the Indian Tree , Chief Redwood dons a feathered headdress and speaks in stereotypical Native American fashion . There is an older , male tree who may or may not be the husband of Madame Willow . There is another female tree whose lips are always in the shape of an `` O '' . There is a baby tree .
