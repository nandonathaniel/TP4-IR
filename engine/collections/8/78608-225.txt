Cody searches for a summer job , and finds a `` help wanted '' sign at the local mini-mart . When the owner realizes Cody is friends with London , he will only hire Cody if he will set him up on a date with her . Meanwhile , Zack repeats English in summer school , but when Zack discovers he is the smartest student in his summer school class , all the other kids give him three hanging wedgies but in the end he tutors them too which causes them to pass the entire summer school class . Zack later finds the wedgies strangely relaxing .
