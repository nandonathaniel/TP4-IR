In the original February 7 , 1998 , broadcast airing of the Superman : The Animated Series episode `` Apokolips ... Now ! Part 2 '' on the Kids ' WB programming block , an animated Funky Flashman was visible mourning the death of Daniel `` Terrible '' Turpin , a character based on his longtime Marvel Comics collaborator Jack Kirby . This shot was later modified to remove the likeness of Lee and other of background Marvel characters when the episode was released on DVD .
