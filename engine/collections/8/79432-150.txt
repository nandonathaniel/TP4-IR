Mormon beliefs in theocracy and polygamy bitterly alienated many ; violent attacks were common and the Mormons were driven out of state after state . Smith was assassinated in 1844 and Brigham Young led the Mormon Exodus from the United States to Mexican territory in Utah in 1847 . They settled the Mormon Corridor . The United States acquired permanent control of this area in 1848 and rejected the Mormons ' 1849 State of Deseret proposal for self - governance , and instead established the Utah Territory in 1850 . Conflicts between Mormons and territorial federal appointees flared , included the Runaway Officials of 1851 ; this eventually led to the small - scale Utah War of 1857 - 1858 , after which Utah remained occupied by Federal troops until 1861 .
