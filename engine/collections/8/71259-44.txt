The major multipurpose project ( Irrigation , Hydropower , Flood Control , development of Fisheries , Tourism and so forth ) built on the river is the Ranjit Sagar Dam ( also known as Thein dam as it is in Thein village ) . The left bank is in Punjab and the right bank is in Jammu and Kashmir . It is located on the main stem of the Ravi River , about 24 kilometres ( 15 mi ) upstream of Madhopur Headworks ( built during pre-partition time ) . The project is an outcome of the development plan conceived for the use of the waters of three eastern rivers allocated to India under the Indus Treaty , namely the Sutlej , the Beas and the Ravi , for irrigation , hydropower generation and other consumptive uses .
