Manchester Airport has two parallel runways . Runway 1 ( 23R / 05L ) 3,048 m × 45 m ( 10,000 ft × 148 ft ) and Runway 2 ( 23L / 05R ) 3,200 m × 45 m ( 10,499 ft × 148 ft ) . The parallel runways lie 390 m ( 1,280 ft ) apart and staggered by 1,850 m ( 6,070 ft ) so that landings can be conducted independently on one runway whilst takeoffs are conducted on the other .
