The cast will include returning actors from both films : Bruce Willis , Samuel L. Jackson , Spencer Treat Clark , and Charlayne Woodard from Unbreakable and James McAvoy and Anya Taylor - Joy from Split will all reprise their respective roles in Glass . Sarah Paulson will be joining the cast as a new character . The film will focus on Dunn ( Willis ) chasing down Crumb ( McAvoy ) in his Beast persona , when Mr. Glass ( Jackson ) is revealed to have masterminded events to both men .
