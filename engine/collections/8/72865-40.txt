The Voice is an American singing competition television series broadcast on NBC . It premiered during the spring television cycle on April 26 , 2011 , and expanded into the fall cycle with the premiere of the third season on September 10 , 2012 . Based on the original The Voice of Holland , it has aired fourteen seasons and aims to find currently unsigned singing talent ( solo or duets , professional and amateur ) contested by aspiring singers , age 15 or over ( age 13 or over since season 12 ) , drawn from public auditions .
