Marco and his team once again fail to trap Meteora and everyone , with the exception of Tom , has their soul drained . Meanwhile , the amnesiac Star and Moon fool around in the Realm of Magic , until the First - Born sends them away . Star then wakes up at home , with Moon still missing . As River holds off Meteora , Star and Tom try to reach the sanctuary to revive Marco , but they are unable to access it . Tom proposes they flee to his realm , but seeing that Meteora has defeated River convinces Star that she must face her . They fight and just as Star is about to be defeated Eclipsa takes her wand and regresses Meteora into a baby . As everyone 's souls return , Star allows Eclipsa to keep the wand . However , she then flees with Meteora and Glossaryck , whose amnesia was just an act . They arrive at the monster temple , where they find Eclipsa 's monster husband , Globgor , inside a crystal prison .
