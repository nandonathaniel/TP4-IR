The protagonist , Lázaro , lives by his wits in an effort to survive and succeed in an impoverished country full of hypocrisy . As a pícaro character , he is an alienated outsider , whose ability to expose and ridicule individuals compromised with society gives him a revolutionary stance . Lázaro states that the motivation for his writing is to communicate his experiences of overcoming deception , hypocrisy , and falsehood ( engaño ) .
