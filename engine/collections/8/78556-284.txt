Steven is put on trial before Blue and Yellow Diamond for Rose Quartz 's assassination of Pink Diamond . During the trial , Steven 's attorney ( a Zircon ) analyses the evidence and argues that the public account of Pink Diamond 's death does n't make sense , and suggests that the true facts of the case have been covered up . Guest stars : Amy Sedaris as the Zircons , Lisa Hannigan as Blue Diamond , and Patti LuPone as Yellow Diamond
