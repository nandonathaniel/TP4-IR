`` There 's No Place I 'd Rather Be '' is a song sung by Singaporean artiste , Kit Chan . Along with Will you , it serves as the official theme to the National Day Parade in 2007 . The single is one of the few National Day Parade themes that does n't mention Singapore 's name . The song was originally written for Singapore Polytechnic 's , `` Musical Superstar : The Pop Musical '' . It was later sung by Project SuperStar finalist , Kelly Poon before being commissioned as an official National Day Parade theme in 2007 .
