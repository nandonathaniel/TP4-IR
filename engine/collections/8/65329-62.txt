Capitals top line winger Tom Wilson was involved in separate controversial plays in Games 2 and 3 . In Game 2 , Wilson made contact with the head of Brian Dumoulin , sandwiching the defenseman between himself and Alex Ovechkin . Dumoulin exited the game , but would return for Game 3 . The next game , Wilson delivered a hard hit to the shoulder of Zach Aston - Reese , giving the rookie center a concussion and broken jaw . Wilson would receive a three game suspension from the NHL Department of Player Safety , and Aston - Reese missed the remainder of the series from the sustained injuries .
