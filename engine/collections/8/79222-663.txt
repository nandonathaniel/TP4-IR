Any factory work , except in delivery and clerical employment in an enclosed office of a factory or in dry cleaning stores , shoe repair shops , and similar service stores . Operating or assisting in operating any machinery unless all moving parts other than keys , levers , or handles are so guarded as to prevent any part of the person or clothing for the operator from touching them . Painting or exterior cleaning in connection with the maintenance of a building or structure . Operating washing , grinding , cutting , slicing , pressing , or mixing machinery . Work at institutions in the Department of Mental Health . Industrial homework . Places of entertainment as a rope or wirewalker or gymnast , unless protected by the use of safety devices or protective equipment that comply with the Federal Occupational Safety and Health Act . Peddling ; drug traffic ; or any practice , exhibition , or place dangerous or injurious to life , limb , or morals . Dangerous farm jobs .
