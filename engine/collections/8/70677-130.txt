The eighth season of The Walking Dead has received positive reviews from critics . On Rotten Tomatoes , the season holds a score of 71 % with an average rating of 6.94 out of 10 , based on 13 reviews , and an average episode score 72 % . The site 's critical consensus reads : `` The Walking Dead 's eighth season energizes its characters with some much - needed angst and action , though it 's still occasionally choppy and lacking forward - moving plot progression .
