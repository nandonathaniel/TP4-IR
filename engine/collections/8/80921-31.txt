Inventor Flint Lockwood and his friends have saved the world from the food storm created by the `` Flint Lockwood Diatonic Super Mutating Dynamic Food Replicator '' ( FLDSMDFR ) but this has left their island home town of Swallow Falls covered in food . Super-inventor Chester V , the CEO of Live Corp and Flint 's childhood hero , offers his company 's services to clean the island and helps to relocate the population to nearby San Franjose , California . Chester also invites Flint to work at Live Corp .
