In 2015 Eurobarometer found that Christianity was the religion for 47.6 % of the respondents , with Protestantism being the main denomination with 36.5 % , followed by other Christians with 8.6 % , Catholics with 1.6 % and Eastern Orthodox with 0.8 % . 31.0 % of the sample declared to be Agnostic and 19.0 % declared to be atheist .
