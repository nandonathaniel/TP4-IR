Popular rap trio The Fat Boys recorded a twelve - inch single titled `` Chillin ' with the Refrigerator '' released on Sutra Records . A novelty hit , `` Frig - O - Rator , '' was released in December 1985 on the Motown label by Roq - In ' Zoo and featured sound bites of Bears game plays . The following year The Fridge was yet again remembered in a rap song , this time by the obscure Los Angeles - based hip - hop group Hard Machine who released the single `` Refrigerator . ''
