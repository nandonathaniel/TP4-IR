On September 30 , 2014 , the CDC announced that Thomas Eric Duncan , a reportedly 42 - year - old ( later corrected by CDC reports as a 45 - year - old ) Liberian national visiting the United States from Liberia , had been diagnosed with Ebola in Dallas , Texas . Duncan , who had been visiting family in Dallas , was treated at Texas Health Presbyterian Hospital Dallas . By October 4 , Duncan 's condition had deteriorated from `` serious but stable '' to `` critical '' . On October 8 , Duncan died of Ebola.
