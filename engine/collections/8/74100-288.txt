At various times during the academic year one of the Faculties or the Student Association sponsors a day - long pilgrimage for students and faculty to locales such as Assisi , Norcia , Cascia , Subiaco , Orvieto , Siena , or Roccasecca , birthplace of St. Thomas Aquinas . Chaplaincy of the Angelicum sponsors a `` Karol Wojtyla Discussion Group '' that meets weekly . The Angelicum Choir meets for practice each week in the Chapel .
