In 1993 , Cher recorded a cover version of `` I Got You Babe '' with the American animated characters Beavis and Butt - Head . The song was the first single from The Beavis and Butt - Head Experience , a compilation comedy album released in 1993 by the Geffen Records , which is one of the fastest - selling comedy albums , has officially sold 1,610,000 units and was certified 2x Platinum by the RIAA in the United States . AllMusic reviewed this song by calling it `` most interesting '' track on the album . The single reached the top 40 in the UK , Belgium and Sweden , as well as the top ten in the Netherlands .
