California Y Open carry legal in rural counties with local ordinances allowing open carry . Some of these counties issue a permit for open carry . Additionally , a person may also open carry if he or she `` reasonably believes that any person or the property of any person is in immediate , grave danger and that the carrying of the weapon is necessary for the preservation of that person or property . '' One can expect to be detained and questioned by law enforcement in most urban areas if using the latter rationale as the basis for openly carrying a firearm in public .
