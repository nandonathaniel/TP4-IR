Premiership of the Century 1967 `` Richmond , under coach Tommy Hafey , finished the 1967 home - and - away season on top . The Tigers disposed of Carlton by 40 points in the second - semi , then faced up to a star - studded Geelong combination in the Grand Final . At the end of a spectacular contest , Richmond had broken a 24 - year premiership drought . Barrot , Brown , Hart , Dean and Bartlett starred , while unsung hero Ronaldson kicked three vital goals .
