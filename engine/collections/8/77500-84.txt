The film was dubbed into Telugu Language with the title `` Premaalayam '' , which gained positive reviews along with the sound tracks , the film became popular . The whole soundtrack was dubbed into Telugu and composed by Raamlaxamn . This track list included only 11 tracks , cutting away 3 tracks from the original . The vocalists included top & eminent singers of Telugu Film Industry - S.P. Balasubrahmanyam , K.S. Chitra & Mano .
