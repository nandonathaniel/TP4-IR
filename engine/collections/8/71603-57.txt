In October 2012 , Megan & Liz covered the song . Walk Off the Earth recorded an a capella version of the song . Their version has received over 30 million views as of November 2016 . Sammy Adams released a remix to the song in November 2012 . Tom Odell covered the song for BBC Radio 1 's Live Lounge . Ariel Winter also recorded a version of the song . Ed Sheeran also made an acoustic rendition of the song . Jessie J covered the song for BBC Radio 1 's Live Lounge . The song has also been covered by rock band The Animal In Me , metal band Past Inhale and metalcore band We Came as Romans , with the latter for Punk Goes Pop 6 . The Veronicas covered the song and meshed it with Mad Love when they did their acoustic tour of America .
