Petrobras has noted on its website several initiatives that it has taken to preserve the environment . These include sponsoring efforts to support both ocean and forest ecosystems . Most notably , Petrobras has sponsored population studies and conservation efforts of humpback whales in the northeast . The company 's efforts have helped to rebuild Brazil 's humpback whale populations from 2,000 in the mid-nineties to over 9,000 in 2008 .
