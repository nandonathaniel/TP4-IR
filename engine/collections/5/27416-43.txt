Filming locations for A Wrinkle in Time were established to be in Central Otago , New Zealand , during the last two weeks of February 2017 . Actors and crew involved were in New Zealand for the two weeks to shoot scenes in the Southern Alps , and shooting took place at Hunter Valley Station near Lake Hāwea , with cast and crew treated to a traditional Māori powhiri and karakia , by the indigenous peoples of New Zealand . Disney wrapped filming in New Zealand 's South Island after two weeks , and the director has declared the cast and crew 's love for New Zealand on Instagram .
