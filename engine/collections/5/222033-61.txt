Creator Liz Tigelaar came up with the idea for the show in 2007 and developed it with director Gary Fleder . Tigelaar and Fleder pitched the show , then titled Light Years , to ABC Studios who accepted it . They then sold the show to The CW . In September 2008 , The CW ordered the show to pilot . After the pilot was ordered , ABC then dropped the show for financial reasons . The show was then picked up by CBS Television Studios . The pilot was filmed in January 2009 , being written by Tigelaar and directed by Fleder . Both also served as executive producers .
