On 4 May , Zidane led Real Madrid to a place in the 2016 UEFA Champions League final by beating Manchester City 1 -- 0 on aggregate . In La Liga , Real Madrid finished runners up , just one point behind Barcelona . In the Champions League final on 28 May , Real Madrid defeated fellow Madrid club Atlético Madrid in a penalty shootout to grab the 11th European Cup ( La Undécima ) in the club history . Zidane became the seventh man to win the European Cup ( now UEFA Champions League ) as both a player and a manager , the second man ( after Miguel Muñoz ) to win the trophy with Real Madrid as both a player and a manager , and the first French manager , except the French - Argentinian Helenio Herrera , to win the trophy .
