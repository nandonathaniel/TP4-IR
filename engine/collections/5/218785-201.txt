On the first season of Law & Order : Criminal Intent , episode `` Homo Homimi Lupus '' , Detective Robert Goren is able to catch and arrest Simon Matic , a former Serbian soldier wanted for extortion and rape by calling Matic 's cellphone after following him into a crowded restaurant . His victim had told Goren that his ringtone was the theme to Bonanza . Matic also goes by the name of `` Little Joe . ''
