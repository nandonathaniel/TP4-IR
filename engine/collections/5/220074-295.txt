Jenna ( ジェンナ ) Voiced by : Himika Akaneya One of the heads of the Druids alongside her sister Zaneri in their sacred land Istar . Unlike her serious sister , Jenna is energetic and show interest of outsiders , even teasing and messing with others . She supervises the physical training of the Seven Deadly Sins in preparation for their battle against the Ten Commandments . It is revealed she and Zaneri are Goddesses who lost their powers in the war 3,000 years ago , but were allowed to borrow the body of the twin sisters that they currently inhabit in order to escape the battlefield .
