Haroun : The main character / central consciousness of the story . A young , curious , courageous , outspoken child . He struggles throughout most of the story with a form of attention - deficit disorder caused by his mother running away with Mr. Sengupta at exactly eleven o'clock , and under its influence he is unable to concentrate for a longer period of time ( not more than eleven minutes ) . But he eventually overcomes his disorder at the climax , never to suffer from it again . He and his father are both named after the `` legendary Caliph of Baghdad , Haroun al - Rashid , who features in many Arabian Nights tales . Their surname Khalifa actually means Caliph ''
