Fox has appeared on many stage productions during her career . She worked in New York City , Alexandria , Silver Spring , and Atlanta , where she lives . She received a Helen Hayes Award nomination for her performance in the 2002 play Home . Her other stage credits include For Colored Girls Who Have Considered Suicide / When the Rainbow Is Enuf , Antony and Cleopatra , The Comedy of Errors , A Raisin in the Sun , The Colored Museum , The Amen Corner , The Boys from Syracuse , Bessie 's Blues , Little Shop of Horrors and The Rocky Horror Picture Show . The was a member of the Alliance Theatre for many years . During 2014 - 15 , she acted as lead in the play , Blues for an Alabama Sky .
