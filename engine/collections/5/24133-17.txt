`` La Vie en rose '' ( French pronunciation : ​ ( la vi ɑ̃ ʁoz ) ) was the signature song of popular French singer Édith Piaf , written in 1945 , popularized in 1946 , and released as a single in 1947 . The song became very popular in the US in 1950 with no fewer than seven different versions reaching the Billboard charts . These were by Tony Martin , Paul Weston , Bing Crosby ( recorded June 22 , 1950 ) , Ralph Flanagan , Victor Young and Louis Armstrong .
