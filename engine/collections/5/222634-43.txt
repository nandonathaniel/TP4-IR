A nice guy at heart , Eric is a stereotypical nerd / geek , scrawny and somewhat clumsy . He is not at all athletic and shows little interest in sports . He is a smart - aleck teenager with a lightning - fast wit and a sarcastic and deadpan sense of humor . He also lights `` incense '' with his friends in his basement . Eric is also known to screw things up a lot , which is parodied throughout the series .
