The entire public transit network and most Boston taxi services were suspended , as was Amtrak service to and from Boston . Logan International Airport remained open under heightened security . Universities , schools , many businesses , and other facilities were closed as thousands of law enforcement personnel participated in the door - to - door manhunt in Watertown , as well as following up other leads , including at the house that the brothers shared in Cambridge . Seven improvised explosive devices were recovered by bomb squads .
