Details of the ritual vary from region to region . Typically the effigy or doll representing Marzanna is made of straw and dressed in traditional local costume , rags , or even bridesmaid 's clothes . Marzanna may resemble a young woman wearing a garland as well as an old crone . Village dwellers carry the effigy away from the village , often singing event - related songs .
