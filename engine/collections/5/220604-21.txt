The Blue Lagoon is a 1980 American romantic survival drama film directed by Randal Kleiser and filmed on the Blue Lagoon in Malta and on Turtle Island in Fiji . The screenplay by Douglas Day Stewart was based on the 1908 novel The Blue Lagoon by Henry De Vere Stacpoole . The film stars Brooke Shields and Christopher Atkins .
