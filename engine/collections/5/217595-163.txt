Riley wonders how she will positively impact the world , and feels she will never be as `` perfect '' as her parents . In exploring this question , she and Maya talk to a nun at an orphanage and a clown at the circus , but Riley is reminded about being in her parents ' shadow when she sees them contributing at those places . Maya suggests Riley tell some stories she remembers about her parents ' young days , and the two see Cory and Topanga at their age . As Riley sees a little of both of her parents in her , and their quirks , she is reminded by the younger Topanga that she is unique .
