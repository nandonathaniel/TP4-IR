The New York Yankees returned to the Fall Classic after being swept by the Cincinnati Reds the previous year . In free agency , the Yankees signed slugging right fielder Reggie Jackson for US $2.96 million ( $12,729,731 in current dollar terms ) over five years and Cincinnati Reds ace pitcher Don Gullett for $2 million ( $8,601,170 in current dollar terms ) over six years . Two other key players were acquired by the Yankees through trades . Shortstop Bucky Dent was picked up from the Chicago White Sox for outfielder Oscar Gamble , pitcher LaMarr Hoyt , and $200,000 . And after only one year with the Oakland Athletics , pitcher Mike Torrez was acquired in exchange for pitcher Dock Ellis and utilitymen Marty Perez and Larry Murray .
