Aesop Rock toured the United States to promote Skelethon . Also , he has been touring with Kimya Dawson and performing material as The Uncluded . Along with playing a number of individual events , The Uncluded performed as part of a Rhymesayers lineup at Summerfest in Milwaukee , Wisconsin On the Skelethon tour , a majority of his touring equipment was stolen , prompting Aesop to raise funds by releasing limited edition artwork . It was announced he will be performing on the first day of Coachella 2013 .
