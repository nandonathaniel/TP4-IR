He contested assembly elections to Uttar Pradesh assembly in November 1993 from two constituencies , namely Atrauli and Kasganj and won from both . As Samajwadi Party - Bahujan Samaj Party combine formed the government in the state under the chief ministership of Mulayam Singh Yadav , even though the BJP won the most votes and increased its vote share Kalyan Singh served as Leader of Opposition in the assembly .
