At yet another screening of the film at Nueplex Cinemas at Rashid Minhas Road , Karachi , protestors were allegedly held in the basement and beaten by the cinema 's private security , with various media reports confirming the incident . The demonstrators ' phones were also confiscated and the cinema 's security allegedly tried to plant incriminating evidence in one of the protestors ' bags in order to make their case appear stronger to police . However , they were let go later after the arrival of Rangers personnel .
