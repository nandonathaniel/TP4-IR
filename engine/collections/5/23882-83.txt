Rondell Sheridan portrayed Victor Baxter , is the father of Raven and Cory , and husband of Tanya . He starts out as a chef in a restaurant , but he later opens his own restaurant , The Chill Grill . His biggest rival is Leonard Stevenson . In Cory in the House , he gets a job as the President 's chef , and it is unknown what became of his restaurant . His catchphrases are `` Here comes the pain ! '' and `` I 'll go pack ! '' ( Cory in the House )
