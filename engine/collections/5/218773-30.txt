Pacific Rim Uprising is an upcoming American science fiction monster film directed by Steven S. DeKnight and written by DeKnight , Emily Carmichael , Kira Snyder , and T.S. Nowlin from a story by DeKnight and Nowlin . It is the sequel to the 2013 film Pacific Rim , and stars John Boyega , Scott Eastwood , Cailee Spaeny , Jing Tian , and Adria Arjona , with Charlie Day , Burn Gorman , and Rinko Kikuchi returning in their roles from the original film . It is scheduled to be released on March 23 , 2018 , by Universal Pictures .
