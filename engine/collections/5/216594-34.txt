Vieira married CBS News journalist Richard M. Cohen on June 14 , 1986 . They reside with their three children in Irvington , Westchester County , New York . Cohen has had multiple sclerosis since he was 25 years old , and has had two bouts of colon cancer , one in 1999 and one a year later . Their younger son , Gabe Cohen , is a reporter for KOMO - TV in Seattle , Washington .
