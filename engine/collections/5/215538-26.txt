The song was featured in the movie Summer Lovers ( 1982 ) , though it was not included on the movie 's soundtrack . It was also featured in the theatrical and VHS releases of National Lampoon 's Vacation , but was replaced with the sonically unrelated `` Little Boy Sweet '' on future versions . Later on , it featured in Hot Shots Part Deux ( 1993 ) and briefly in Eddie Murphy 's version of The Nutty Professor ( 1996 ) . Other films that featured the song include Protocol ( 1984 ) , Playing for Keeps ( 1986 ) , Riff - Raff ( 1991 ) , The Story of Us ( 1999 ) , Transformers : Revenge of the Fallen ( 2009 ) , and I 'm So Excited ( 2011 ) which also takes it name from the song itself .
