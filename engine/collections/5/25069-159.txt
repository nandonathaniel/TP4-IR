At the end of 2008 , East Jerusalem 's main Arab neighborhoods included Shuafat ( 38,800 ) , Beit Hanina ( 27,900 ) , the Muslim Quarter of the Old City ( 26,300 ) , At - Tur including As - Sawana ( 24,400 ) . East Jerusalem 's main Jewish neighborhoods include Ramot ( 42,200 ) , Pisgat Ze'ev ( 42,100 ) , Gilo ( 26,900 ) , Neve Yaakov ( 20,400 ) , Ramat Shlomo ( 15,100 ) and East Talpiot ( 12,200 ) . The Old City ( including the already mentioned Muslim Quarter ) has an Arab population of 36,681 and a Jewish population of 3,847 .
