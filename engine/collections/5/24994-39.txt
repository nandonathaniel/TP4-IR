Theo Theodorakis is a smart high school student , and very loyal to his family . He is protective of his brother Chris , and works hard in his parents ' coffee shop . He is interested in becoming a writer , and also becomes friends with his partner , Doug Hoo . Doug Hoo , son of Jimmy Hoo , is a high school track star , one of the best mile - runners in the state . Running is his passion , but his father often criticizes him for not studying . At the end , he wins two Olympic gold medals and sets the 1500m record as well
