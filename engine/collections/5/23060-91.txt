Jamaica Workers who have worked up to 220 days enjoy 10 days maximum ( if they have worked for a minimum of 110 days ) calculated as follows ; 1 / 22 of the number of days of work . Any fraction of a day of holiday shall be reckoned as 1 day . For workers who have worked more than 220 days : 2 working weeks . For workers with 10 years of service or more and who have worked more than 220 days in each qualifying year : 3 working weeks . 10
