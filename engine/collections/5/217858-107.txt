The Upper Rhine region was changed significantly by a Rhine straightening program in the 19th Century . The rate of flow was increased and the ground water level fell significantly . Dead branches were removed by construction workers and the area around the river was made more habitable for humans flood plains as flooding decreased sharply . On the French side , the Grand Canal d'Alsace was dug , which carries a significant part of the river water , and all of the traffic . In some places , there are large compensation pools , for example , the huge Bassin de compensation de Plobsheim in Alsace .
