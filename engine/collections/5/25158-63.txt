No reliable data is available on the box office earnings of Mother India . It was in continuous distribution in theatres in India until the mid-1990s . There was a renewed interest in the film in the 1970s causing an upsurge in ticket sales . Film trade websites provide estimates of its business . Boxofficeindia.com gives ₹ 40 million ( US $600,000 ) as its gross , and ₹ 1,173 million ( US $17 million ) as its adjusted net , as of 2012 . According to Chatterjee , it did exceptionally good business in Delhi , Uttar Pradesh , Gujarat , Karnataka ( then called Mysore State ) and Maharashtra . The film 's success led Khan to name his next film Son of India . Released in 1962 , it was not well received .
