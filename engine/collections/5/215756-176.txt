In Jammu , Hindus constitute 62.55 % of the population , Muslims 33.45 % and Sikhs , 3.3 % ; In Ladakh ( comprises Buddhists - dominated Leh and Shia Muslim - dominated Kargil ) , Muslims constitute about 46.4 % of the population , the remaining being Buddhists ( 39.7 % ) and Hindus ( 12.1 % ) . The people of Ladakh are of Indo - Tibetan origin , while the southern area of Jammu includes many communities tracing their ancestry to the nearby Indian states of Haryana and Punjab , as well as the city of Delhi .
