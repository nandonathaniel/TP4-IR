Three chime R.V.B horns have also been fitted to the older Electric Multiple Units ( EMU 's ) operating in Victoria . The old Swing Door ( train ) and Tait ( train ) EMU 's of the 1920s and 1930s , were fitted with R.V.B three chimes , while the Harris ( train ) ( 1950 's & 60 's ) , Hitachi ( train ) ( 1970 's ) , and Comeng ( train ) ( 1980 's ) were also fitted with R.V.B three chime horns .
