Timothy Treadwell , 46 , male Amie Huguenard , 37 , female October 5 , 2003 Wild Katmai National Park , Alaska Treadwell and Huguenard 's corpses were found by their pilot at Kaflia Bay . Treadwell was famous for his books and documentaries on living with wild bears in Alaska . State Troopers investigating the incident recovered an audiotape of the attack . The two were killed on the last night before their scheduled pickup after spending several months in the Alaskan bush . The attack is chronicled in the 2005 American documentary film Grizzly Man by German director Werner Herzog .
