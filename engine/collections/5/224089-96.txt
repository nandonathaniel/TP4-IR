Other known binary compounds include halides ( LiF , LiCl , LiBr , LiI ) , sulfide ( Li S ) , superoxide ( LiO ) , and carbide ( Li ) . Many other inorganic compounds are known in which lithium combines with anions to form salts : borates , amides , carbonate , nitrate , or borohydride ( LiBH ) . Lithium aluminium hydride ( LiAlH ) is commonly used as a reducing agent in organic synthesis .
