That night , Elise and Tucker hear the whistle being blown downstairs . Elise follows the sound into the basement , where she realizes it is coming from behind a wall . Elise asks a series of questions and learns that what she is looking for is behind the wall . Elise sees that the wall is a door and rushes to her bedroom to get the keys . Behind the door , she finds a bloodied girl with a chain around her neck . Ted storms into the room and it 's shown that he was keeping the girl against her will . He locks the group in the room and tries to kill Specs , but he tricks Ted , and kills him with a bookcase . The police rescue the group and Elise is taken in for questioning .
