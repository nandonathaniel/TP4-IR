On 25 November , Ibrahimović captained PSG on his return to hometown club Malmö FF in the group stage of the 2015 -- 16 Champions League . He scored the team 's third goal in a 5 -- 0 win , a result that confirmed PSG 's qualification to the knockout stage of the competition . In scoring twice in PSG 's 3 -- 0 win over Nice on 4 December 2015 , Ibrahimović beat Mustapha Dahleb 's previous record of 85 goals to become the club 's all - time top goalscorer in the French first division .
