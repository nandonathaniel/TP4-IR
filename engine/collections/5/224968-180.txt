Arkansas , like most other Southern states , is part of the Bible Belt and is predominantly Protestant . The largest denominations by number of adherents in 2010 were the Southern Baptist Convention with 661,382 ; the United Methodist Church with 158,574 ; non-denominational Evangelical Protestants with 129,638 ; and the Catholic Church with 122,662 . There are some residents of the state who live by other religions such as Wiccan , Pagan , Islam , Hinduism , Buddhism or who claim no religious affiliation .
