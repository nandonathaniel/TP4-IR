After India independence in 1947 , Devi Lal emerged as a leader of farmers . During his two tenures as chief minister of Haryana he made several decisions benefiting farmers and rural people . His popularity among farmers and rural people , earned him the title of ' Tau ' ( Elder Uncle ) . Devi Lal died on 6 April 2001 at the age of 85 . He was cremated at Sangarsh Sthal on the banks of the river Yamuna in New Delhi . `` Kisan Ghat '' is the samadhi of another popular leader of the farmers , Charan Singh , fifth Prime Minister of India .
