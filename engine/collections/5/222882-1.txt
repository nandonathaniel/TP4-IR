Revolvers still remain popular as back - up and off - duty handguns among American law enforcement officers and security guards and are still common in the American private sector as defensive and sporting / hunting firearms . Famous and iconic revolvers models include the Webley , the Colt Single Action Army , the Colt Official Police , Smith & Wesson Model 10 , the Smith and Wesson Model 29 of Dirty Harry fame , and the Nagant M1895 .
