A former drug courier who became a US Federal agent to bring down the Mexican drug cartel he worked for . It is soon revealed that the detective who arrested him was the one who betrayed him to the same drug cartel . After failing to save his brother , Raul is trying to make amends with his niece , Nadia ; both are unaware that they are biological father and daughter . Gift : mind - reading and telepathy ; side - effect : headaches .
