Madrid boasts a prominent place in Spanish basketball , with two clubs in the country 's top - level Liga ACB. Real Madrid 's basketball section has won a record 9 Euroleague Championships , 33 Spanish Leagues and 27 Spanish Cups , having achieved 3 Triple Crowns . Madrid 's other professional basketball club is Estudiantes that have won 3 Spanish Cup championships . Both teams play their home games at the Palacio de Deportes .
