Sea of Thieves is a shared world action - adventure video game developed by Rare and published by Microsoft Studios for Microsoft Windows 10 and Xbox One . The game allows players to take the role of a pirate , sailing the seas of a fantastical world either solo or as part of a crew of up to four players . The game features both co-operative and player versus player combat . It was released worldwide on 20 March 2018 and received mixed reviews from critics .
