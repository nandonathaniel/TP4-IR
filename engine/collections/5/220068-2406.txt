Previously Washington University , was renamed weeks after Robert E. Lee died as the President of the university . A large Confederate battle flag and a number of related flags were removed from the Chapel in 2014 . Inside Lee Chapel , in place of an altar , is a `` gigantic '' marble statue of Lee , recumbent , wearing Confederate battle gear and resting on a camp bed . ( Lee is buried with his family in a mausoleum beneath the chapel . ) Robert E. Lee Residence . Grave of Traveller , Robert E. Lee 's horse ( 1871 ) . Apples are regularly placed on the grave by visitors .
