Hispanic or Latino of any race were 11.7 % of the population in 2010 : 9.7 % were of Mexican , 0.2 % Puerto Rican , 0.1 % Cuban , and 1.7 % other Hispanic or Latino origin . According to the 2010 -- 2015 American Community Survey , the largest ancestry groups were German ( 19.4 % ) , Irish ( 12.0 % ) , English ( 11.6 % ) , American ( 5.5 % ) , and Norwegian ( 3.9 % ) .
