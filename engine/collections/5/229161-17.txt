WNV occurs in Europe , the Middle East , Africa , India , Asia , Australia , and North America . In the United States thousands of cases are reported a year , with most occurring in August and September . It can occur in outbreaks of disease . The virus was discovered in Uganda in 1937 and was first detected in North America in 1999 . Severe disease may also occur in horses and a vaccine for these animals is avaliable . A surveillance system in birds is useful for early detection of a potential human outbreak .
