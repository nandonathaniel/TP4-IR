The conniving Dirk Manus runs into a lot of trouble from Marissa Faireborn and the Technobots . While Dirk is in custody , it is revealed that he somehow got a hold of a dangerous weapon and intended to sell it to the highest bidder . It also turns out that the Quintessons expressed the most interest in it , but they did n't pay for it . Now , Marissa , the Technobots , and Dirk need to get the weapon back from the Quintessons before it 's too late .
