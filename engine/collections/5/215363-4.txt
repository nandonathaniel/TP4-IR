2,116 UK military and civilian personnel were admitted to UK Field Hospitals and categorised as Wounded in Action , including as a result of hostile action . 4,529 UK military and civilian personnel were admitted to UK Field Hospitals for disease or non-battle injuries . 293 UK personnel were categorised as Very Seriously Injured from all causes excluding disease . 298 UK personnel were categorised as Seriously Injured from all causes excluding disease . 6,663 UK personnel were aeromedically evacuated from Afghanistan on medical grounds , for whatever reason .
