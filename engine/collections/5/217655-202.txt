Formula One has an extensive web following , with most major TV companies covering it such as the BBC . The official Formula One website ( formula1.com ) has a live timing JavaScript applet that can be used during the race to keep up with the leaderboard in real time . Recently an official application has been made available in the iTunes App Store and on Google Play that allows iOS and Android users to see a real time feed of driver positions , timing and commentary . The same official application has been available for Android phones and tablets since 2011 .
