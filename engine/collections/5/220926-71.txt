Sipowicz and Clark investigate a serial killer who infiltrates AA meetings and preys on female members by strangling them and smudging their lipstick as his trademark . Ortiz and Murphy go undercover in a sting operation to catch the AA killer . A man is found stabbed to death in his apartment , which is full of steroids , and the investigation leads Clark to face the woman he slept with earlier and wrecked a murder case over .
