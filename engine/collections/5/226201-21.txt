MIA hosted the 18th World Congress of Accountants from 8 -- 11 November 2010 , which was held at the Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre . A record number of 6,000 delegates from 134 countries participated . The Congress , which originated in 1904 and has been held regularly since 1977 , was jointly organised by the MIA and the International Federation of Accountants ( IFAC ) .
