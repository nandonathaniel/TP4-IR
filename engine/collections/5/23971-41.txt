The song has appeared in multiple films and television series . It was included in films such as Loser ( 2000 ) , Big Fat Liar ( 2002 ) , Iron Man 3 ( 2013 ) , Mommy ( 2014 ) , and Smurfs : The Lost Village ( 2017 ) , the third instance during a flashback scene set in 1999 , the year the song became popular . In television , it was used in Daria episode The F Word and in 90210 finale of season 1 . On 15 March 2011 Ozone Entertainment released the song through the Rock Band Network . It is the first song on the service to incorporate the keyboard introduced in Rock Band 3 . The song was also featured in an EDF Energy advertisement in 2015 .
