On November 5 , 2015 , after being inducted along with Tom Griswold into the National Radio Hall of Fame , Bob announced his retirement effective at the end of 2015 . His last live show as co-host aired on December 17 , 2015 . On November 17 , 2016 Bob and Tom were reunited when they were inducted into the Indiana Broadcasters Hall of Fame . Bob made his first appearance on the show , as a guest host , since his retirement on April 3 , 2017 when the show is in Cincinnati for the Cincinnati Reds home opener .
