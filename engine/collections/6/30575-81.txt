In 1995 , an established journal of classical scholarship , Classics Ireland , published punk musician 's Iggy Pop 's reflections on the applicability of The Decline and Fall of the Roman Empire to the modern world in a short article , Caesar Lives , ( Vol. 2 , 1995 ) in which he noted `` America is Rome . Of course , why should n't it be ? We are all Roman children , for better or worse ... I learn much about the way our society really works , because the system - origins - military , religious , political , colonial , agricultural , financial - are all there to be scrutinised in their infancy . I have gained perspective . ''
