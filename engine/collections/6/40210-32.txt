A hardworking Monica is introduced in the pilot as one of five close - knit friends who live in New York City , including her older brother Ross ( David Schwimmer ) , neighbors Joey ( Matt Leblanc ) and Chandler ( Matthew Perry ) , and former roommate Phoebe ( Lisa Kudrow ) . When her privileged , inexperienced childhood best friend Rachel ( Jennifer Aniston ) , with whom she had long lost contact , suddenly arrives in her neighborhood unannounced as a runaway bride after abandoning her own wedding , Monica allows her to move in with her while she attempts to reorganize her life , and the two reconnect .
