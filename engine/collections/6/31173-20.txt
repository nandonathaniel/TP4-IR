Coney Island Kalamazoo was founded in 1915 , and is the longest continuously operated Coney Island in the state . Their coney island is made up of a topping made from their own recipe served on a Koegel 's Skinless Frankfurter . Koegel 's was n't founded until 1916 , and it 's unknown which hot dog Coney Island Kalamazoo used prior to the Skinless Frankfurter 's development .
