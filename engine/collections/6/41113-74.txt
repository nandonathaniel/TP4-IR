The events of the 1899 strike later inspired the 1992 Disney film Newsies , including a character named Kid Blink ( who wears an eye patch ) , but in this version of the story the leader of the strike was named Jack Kelly . A musical theatre adaptation of the film , also called Newsies , debuted in 2011 and played on Broadway from 2012 to 2014 , starring Jeremy Jordan as Jack Kelly , and on tour from 2014 - 2016 . A live filmed version of the stage production with cast members from both the Broadway and Tour productions , was digitally released on May 23 , 2017 .
