September 4 , 2010 -- Hurricane Earl passed about 90 miles offshore , but still brought heavy rain , large waves , and tropical storm force gusts to Cape Cod . The heaviest rain affected areas such as Nantucket , Martha 's Vineyard , and areas over Maine , while the strongest wind was a recorded gust of 58 mph near Hyannis , Massachusetts . Sustained winds were of 29 to 35 miles per hour , just at and below tropical storm force .
