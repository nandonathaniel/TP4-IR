2017 Houston Astros Los Angeles Dodgers 5 -- 1 Dodger Stadium , Los Angeles First instance of back - to - back game sevens since 2001 -- 2002 . First World Series championship for the Astros , who became the first AL team to win a World Series game 7 on the road since 1972 . The Astros became the second team to win two winner - take - all game sevens in the same postseason since the Kansas City Royals in 1985 .
