Attack on Titan : No Regrets ( 進撃 の 巨人 悔い なき 選択 , Shingeki no Kyojin Kuinaki Sentaku ) is a spinoff manga written by Gun Snark and illustrated by Hikaru Suruga , serialized in the Aria magazine from its start in the November 28 , 2013 issue to June 28 , 2014 . The manga is an adaptation of the visual novel of the same name . It focuses on the origin story of Levi , before he was a part of the Survey Corps Special Operations Squad . In North America , the series has been licensed in English by Kodansha USA , which released both volumes in 2014 .
