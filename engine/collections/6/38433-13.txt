Vyjayanthimala Bali ( born 13 August 1936 ) , also known by the mononym Vyjayanthimala , is an Indian film actress , Bharathanatyam dancer , Carnatic singer , dance choreographer and parliamentarian . She was referred to as the first female superstar of Hindi cinema . She made her debut in the Tamil language film Vazhkai in 1949 and in the Telugu film Jeevitham in 1950 . She later became one of the most prominent actresses of South Indian cinema and in the golden era of Bollywood and was known as one of the iconic leading actress of all time .
