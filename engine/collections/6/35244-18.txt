Like other wrestling halls of fame , such as the WWE , TNA , and WCW halls of fame , the Wrestling Observer Newsletter Hall of Fame is not a physical place . Nonetheless , it is a respected honor in the world of wrestling . Every year , Meltzer conducts a poll of selected `` insiders '' and wrestlers to determine new inductees into the WON Hall of Fame . Pro Wrestling Illustrated has adopted the WON Hall of Fame as their own .
