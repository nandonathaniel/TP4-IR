Grand Aquarium ( 海洋 奇觀 ) -- Inside the Grand Aquarium , visitors can get up close to some 5,000 fish from over 400 species , such as the scalloped hammerhead and manta ray . Strolling through the Reef Tunnel and Panoramic Ocean Gallery , visitors can see the world 's largest viewing dome in an aquarium , at 5.5 metres in diameter , and a 13 - metre acrylic viewing panel , one of the largest in the world . It also features the world 's first and only 360 ° water screen show Symbio ! and Hong Kong 's only restaurant inside an aquarium .
