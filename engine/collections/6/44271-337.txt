Meanwhile , the men who controlled the Jacobins rejected the Revolutionary Republican Women as dangerous rabble - rousers . At this point the Jacobins controlled the government ; they dissolved the Society of Revolutionary Republican Women , and decreed that all women 's clubs and associations were illegal . They sternly reminded women to stay home and tend to their families by leaving public affairs to the men . Organised women were permanently shut out of the French Revolution after October 30 , 1793 .
