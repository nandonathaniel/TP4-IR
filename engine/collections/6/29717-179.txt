With the sanctuary all sludged up , Star and Queen Moon flee and find a new hiding place at Buff Frog 's . Star wants to sneak out to fight , so she gets Buff Frog to distract Moon with some Mewnian board games such as Puddle Defender , which Buff and Moon find have a biased perspective towards the monsters instead of the Mewnians . After a heated argument , Buff Frog sides with Moon and forbids Star from leaving . However , Buff 's youngest tadpole Katerina later sneaks Star out .
