Hayashi rice ( ハヤシライス ) - beef and onions stewed in a red - wine sauce and served on rice Nikujaga - soy - flavored meat and potato stew that has been made in Japan to the extent that it is now considered washoku , but again originates from 19th Century Japanese Navy chefs adapting beef stews of the Royal Navy . Omu raisu - ketchup - flavored rice wrapped in omelet .
