In 1998 , they performed on Frank Wildhorn 's concept album of the musical The Civil War . The group covered the 1968 Orpheus hit `` Ca n't Find the Time '' for the soundtrack of the Jim Carrey movie Me , Myself & Irene ( 2001 ) . Orpheus creator and the song 's writer Bruce Arnold traded verses with Darius on several occasions , when the band played live on the West Coast . The band had an extensive touring schedule , including an annual New Year 's Eve show at Silverton Las Vegas ( formerly known as Boomtown Las Vegas ) in Enterprise , Nevada . In 2008 , the band started releasing their concerts as downloads through trueAnthem . In 2009 , Hootie & the Blowfish performed live in a ballet which chronicled their rise and success in the 1990s .
