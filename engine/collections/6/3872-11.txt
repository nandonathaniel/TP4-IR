Many familiar mammal species are crepuscular , including some bats , hamsters , housecats , stray dogs , rabbits , ferrets , guinea pigs and rats . Other crepuscular mammals include jaguars , ocelots , bobcats , strepsirrhines , red pandas , bears , deer , moose , sitatunga , capybaras , chinchillas , the common mouse , skunks , Australian wombats , wallabies , quolls , possums and marsupial gliders , tenrecidae , spotted hyenas , and African wild dogs .
