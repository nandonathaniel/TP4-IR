The Sui Dynasty ( Chinese : 隋 朝 ; pinyin : Suí cháo ) was a short - lived imperial dynasty of China of pivotal significance . The Sui unified the Northern and Southern dynasties and reinstalled the rule of ethnic Han Chinese in the entirety of China proper , along with sinicization of former nomadic ethnic minorities ( the Five Barbarians ) within its territory . It was succeeded by the Tang dynasty , which largely inherited its foundation .
