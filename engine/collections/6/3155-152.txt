As many of the tribes had had previous friendly experiences with British and French fur traders in various isolated encounters along the Missouri and Columbia Rivers , the expedition subsequently did not encounter any hostilities with the exception of the Teton - Sioux tribe under Black Buffalo and the Partisan tribe on September 25 , 1804 . Both of these tribes were rivals and hoped to use the expedition to their own advantage and who both demanded tribute from the expedition for their passage over the river at that particular juncture .
