Homo rhodesiensis is the species name proposed by Arthur Smith Woodward ( 1921 ) to classifiy Kabwe 1 ( the `` Kabwe skull '' or `` Broken Hill skull '' , also `` Rhodesian Man '' ) , a fossil recovered from a cave at Broken Hill , or Kabwe , Northern Rhodesia ( now Zambia ) . The fossil has been dated to the late - Middle Pleistocene , between roughly 300,000 and 125,000 years ago .
