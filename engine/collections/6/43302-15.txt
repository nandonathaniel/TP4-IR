Claire is presented to Colum MacKenzie , laird of the castle , and realises she is suspected of being an English spy . She meets Geillis Duncan , and witnesses Jamie doing a kindness for Laoghaire , the pretty young granddaughter of Mrs. Fitzgibbons . As Claire plans to return to Inverness and the standing stones , Colum decides it is best to keep her at Leoch , against her will , as healer to the clan .
