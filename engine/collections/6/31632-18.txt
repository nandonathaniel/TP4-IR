In 2009 , Marsden also played the male lead in the film The Box , based on the 1970 short story `` Button , Button '' by author Richard Matheson , which was earlier made into an episode of The Twilight Zone . He starred opposite Cameron Diaz and reunited with Superman Returns co-star Frank Langella . In 2010 , Marsden was cast in the comedy Death at a Funeral along with Chris Rock , Luke Wilson , Danny Glover , and Columbus Short , which was a remake of the 2007 British film of the same name .
