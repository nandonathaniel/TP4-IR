Immediately after Thrilla in Manilla , Ali summoned Frazier 's son Marvis to his dressing room and apologized for the things he had said about Frazier . When Marvis conveyed Ali 's contrition to him , Frazier commented that Ali should have communicated this to him directly . After returning to the US , Ali called Butch Lewis , and asked for Frazier 's private number , saying he wanted to apologize to Frazier . Lewis then conveyed this request to Frazier , but was told not to share it with Ali , according to Lewis .
