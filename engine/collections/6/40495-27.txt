During the first 56 years of the Grammys , only six artists took the Record of the Year and Best New Artist awards during the same ceremony : Bobby Darin ( `` Mack the Knife '' ) , Christopher Cross ( `` Sailing '' ) , Sheryl Crow ( `` All I Wanna Do '' ) , Norah Jones ( `` Do n't Know Why '' ) , Amy Winehouse ( `` Rehab '' ) and Sam Smith ( `` Stay with Me '' ) .
