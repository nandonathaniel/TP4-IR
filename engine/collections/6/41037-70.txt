While the Tepanecs of Azcapotzalco expanded their kingdom with help from the Mexica , the Acolhua city of Texcoco grew in power in the eastern portion of the lake basin . Eventually , war erupted between the two states , and the Mexica played a vital role in the conquest of Texcoco . By then , Tenochtitlan had grown into a major city and was rewarded for its loyalty to the Tepanecs by receiving Texcoco as a tributary province .
