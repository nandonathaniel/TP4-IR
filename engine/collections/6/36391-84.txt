Five Nights at Freddy 's : The Twisted Ones is the second novel written by Cawthon and Breed - Wrisley . A sequel to Five Nights at Freddy 's : The Silver Eyes , the book was discovered on Amazon under Cawthon 's name on January 8 , 2017 . The discovery sparked controversy on the legitimacy of the product , but Cawthon confirmed that it was official shortly after . The novel was released in some bookstores earlier than planned , but released publicly on June 27 , 2017 . The plot involves Charlie , the main character from The Silver Eyes , who is `` drawn back into the world of her father 's frightening creations '' trying to move on .
