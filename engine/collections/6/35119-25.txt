Karen Blixen recalls her life in Africa where in 1913 she , as an unmarried wealthy Danish woman , is spurned by her Swedish nobleman lover , and moves to Nairobi , British East Africa to complete a marriage of convenience with her lover 's brother , Baron Bror Blixen ( Klaus Maria Brandauer ) . Bror has gone through his money and is reduced to seducing the servant girls ; the couple plans to establish a dairy cattle farm . En route to Nairobi , Blixen meets Denys Finch Hatton ( Robert Redford ) , a local big - game hunter .
