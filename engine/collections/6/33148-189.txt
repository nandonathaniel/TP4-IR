Mandy is ecstatic when she lands her dream summer internship at DKNY in New York City , only to sadly announce later that due to her GPA dropping down , she lost it . However , even after Mike helps her get another internship in New York , she tells her parents she will not take it . Mike and Vanessa eventually discover that Mandy never lost the first internship and the reason she does not want to go is because Kyle offered to follow her there .
