Mithila made her acting debut in the year 2014 through a short film Majha Honeymoon , which was showcased in the 16th Mumbai Film Festival . In Bollywood , her first film was Nikhil Advani 's Katti - Batti , in which she played the role of Imran Khan 's sister . She also sang a song called `` Maharashtra Desha '' in association with Bhartiya Digital Party ( BhaDiPa ) which was released on 1 May 2016 on YouTube , on the occasion of Maharashtra day .
