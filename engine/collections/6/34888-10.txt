In The Return of the King , she disguised herself as a man and , under the alias of Dernhelm ( from Old English dern meaning `` secret , concealed '' ) , travelled with the Riders of Rohan to the Battle of the Pelennor Fields outside the White City of Minas Tirith in Gondor , carrying with her Meriadoc Brandybuck , who had also been ordered to remain behind , on her horse Windfola .
