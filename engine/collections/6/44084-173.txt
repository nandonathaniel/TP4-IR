The principles have been acknowledged with influencing the French proposed UN declaration on sexual orientation and gender identity , which focuses on ending violence , criminalisation and capital punishment and does not include dialogue about same - sex marriage or right to start a family . The proposal was supported by 67 of the then 192 member countries of the United Nations , including all EU member states and the United States . An alternative statement opposing the proposal was initiated by Syria and signed by 57 member nations , including all 27 nations of the Arab League as well as Iran and North Korea .
