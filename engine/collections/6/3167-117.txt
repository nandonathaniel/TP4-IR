Pocahontas became the first Native American Disney Princess and the first woman of color to be the lead character in a Disney film . A video game entitled Disney 's Pocahontas based on the film was released on the Sega Genesis / Mega Drive on January 1 , 1996 . Pocahontas was followed by a direct - to - video sequel entitled Pocahontas II : Journey to a New World ( 1998 ) . Bedard and Kuhn reprised their roles as Pochantas ' speaking and singing voices , respectively , while John Smith was played by Mel Gibson 's brother Donal Gibson . Pocahontas , alongside other Disney Princesses , is set to cameo in the film Ralph Breaks the Internet : Wreck - It Ralph 2 ( 2018 ) , with Bedard returning to the role .
