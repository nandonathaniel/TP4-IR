San Diego Mayor Kevin Faulconer was considered a potential candidate , but during his re-election campaign announced that he would not run for governor in 2018 . However , as of January 2017 , it has been reported that Faulconer was reconsidering his decision and has met with former Mayor of Los Angeles Richard Riordan , who was a 2002 candidate for Governor of California to discuss a potential gubernatorial candidacy . On June 30 , 2017 , Faulconer posted a statement to his Facebook page officially ruling out a gubernatorial bid .
