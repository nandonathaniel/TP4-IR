Black Dwarf is a member of Thanos ' Black Order . Thanos chose him for his immense strength and unbreakable skin . He is also the brother of fellow member Corvus Glaive . He was sent on a mission by Thanos to attack earth by starting in the nation of Wakanda . However , to his shock the people of that nation turned out to be rebellious and he was humiliated . For his failure , Thanos kicked him out of the Black Order . He rejoined the Order to fight the Avengers , but was later killed by Ronan the Accuser .
