Foles was given his third start of the season for Week 9 against the Oakland Raiders . Against the Raiders , Foles threw for seven touchdowns , tying a record held by six other quarterbacks . Foles is one of three quarterbacks to throw seven touchdowns and zero interceptions ; he also amassed a perfect passer rating ( 158.3 ) , having thrown more touchdown passes than incompletions . Foles was awarded his second NFC Offensive Player of the Week Award of the season for his performance against the Raiders .
