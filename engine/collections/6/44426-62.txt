The design for the reverse of the 2020 $10 bill will feature the heroines of the Women 's Suffrage Movement in the United States , including Susan B. Anthony , Alice Paul , Sojourner Truth , Elizabeth Cady Stanton , Lucretia Mott , and the participants of the 1913 Woman Suffrage Procession who marched in Washington D.C. in favor of full voting rights for American women .
