Norway July 4 , 1991 , amended August 20 , 2004 Banned The following breeds are forbidden to give , sell , breed or import , including those in embryonic form , but dogs bred before the law came into effect are legal to possess : Pit Bull Terrier American Staffordshire Terrier Fila Brasileiro Tosa Inu ( incorrectly listed as `` Toso '' ) Dogo Argentino Czechoslovakian Wolfdog and any wolf hybrid Dogs of these breeds that are kept legal , also have to be microchipped .
