The Heroes Trophy has been awarded to the winner of the Iowa - Nebraska game since the 2011 season . `` The Heroes Game , '' as it is known , takes place on Black Friday and is last game of the regular season . The teams have met 48 times , with the series dating back to 1891 , a 22 -- 0 Iowa win . The Cornhuskers lead the series 29 -- 16 - 3 . Iowa currently holds the Heroes Trophy , having defeated the Cornhuskers in 2017 . The teams play annually and will meet next on November 23 , 2018 , at Kinnick Stadium .
