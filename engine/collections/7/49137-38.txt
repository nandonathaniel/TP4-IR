Blue jeans were popularized as work clothes in the 1850s by merchant Levi Strauss , a Jewish - German immigrant in San Francisco , and adopted by many American teenagers a century later . They are now widely worn on every continent by people of all ages and social classes . Along with mass - marketed informal wear in general , blue jeans are arguably U.S. culture 's primary contribution to global fashion . The country is also home to the headquarters of many leading designer labels such as Ralph Lauren and Calvin Klein .
