In an attempt to `` grow up '' for Robbie , Georgia then goes to have `` snogging lessons '' with Peter Dyer . He becomes infatuated with her and at a party the following night , he tries to kiss her , causing her to fall in to a bush and expose her underwear to Robbie , Lindsay , Jas and Tom , who see her on the ground with Peter . Georgia conspires to meet them at the pool and brings her sister along as she is stuck babysitting her . While in the pool Robbie and Georgia kiss . Robbie then leaves , claiming that he has to `` sort some things out '' but that he will phone Georgia later .
