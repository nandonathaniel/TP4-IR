As winners of the Champions League , Inter played in the 2010 UEFA Super Cup against 2009 -- 10 UEFA Europa League winners Atlético Madrid . They lost the match 2 -- 0 , with goals from José Antonio Reyes and Sergio Agüero . They also took part in the 2010 FIFA Club World Cup , entering at the semi-final stage . There they beat 2010 AFC Champions League winners Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma 3 -- 0 to reach the final against 2010 CAF Champions League winners TP Mazembe , whom they also beat 3 -- 0 to take the title .
