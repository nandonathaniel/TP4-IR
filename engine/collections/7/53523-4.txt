In 1896 , an article named `` A Modern Jonah Proves his Story . '' was published in the New York World quoted a brief portion of this story , as told by Rev. William Justin Harsha , along with some initial observations . This was followed about a week later by another article that briefly summarised some responses from readers , followed by a third article by William L. Stone , who related a similar story involving a massive `` man - eating shark '' .
