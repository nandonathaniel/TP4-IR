Irene Adler is a retired American opera singer and actress who appears in `` A Scandal in Bohemia '' . Although this is her only appearance , she is one of the most notable female characters in the stories : the only woman who has ever challenged Holmes intellectually , and one of only a handful of people who ever bested him in a battle of wits .
