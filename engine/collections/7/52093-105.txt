Davies 's script for the second episode finished with the Tenth Doctor 's final line , `` I do n't want to go '' , followed by action text describing the regeneration and ending with the words , `` And there he is . Blinking . Dazed . The New Man . '' He then sent the script to his successor Steven Moffat , who is responsible for all of the Eleventh Doctor 's dialogue that follows . Moffat , as incoming executive producer , also assisted in the production of the final scene .
