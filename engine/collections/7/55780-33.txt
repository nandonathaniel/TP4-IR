The player and partner get quite excited when Chatot gives them an exploration mission , to explore an `` unexplored cave '' called Waterfall Cave . Before this , Chimecho provides a new feature to the exploration team , allowing them to recruit new members . After Waterfall Cave , the player , after piecing together several visions , finds out that the guildmaster , Wigglytuff , had previously explored this cave but forgot about it prior to telling them to explore it .
