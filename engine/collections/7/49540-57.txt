Nerd War : The teams are challenged to a cosplay competition , requiring them to develop a coherent mythology and appearance for the given themes ( villains from a fantasy setting , and heroes from a science fiction setting ) . The teams are judged by actor and author George Takei , costume designer and professional cosplayer Yaya Han , and actor Adam Busch . Reward : The winners train with a world - renowned swords - mistress and are given a Little Caesars pizza lunch .
