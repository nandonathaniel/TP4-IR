Archaeplastida , also known as Plastida or Primoplantae Plantae sensu lato Plants in a broad sense comprise the green plants listed above plus Rhodophyta ( red algae ) and Glaucophyta ( glaucophyte algae ) . This clade includes the organisms that eons ago acquired their chloroplasts directly by engulfing cyanobacteria ( e.g. , Plantae Cavalier - Smith , 1981 ) .
