King David is the husband of Queen Snow White , father of Princess Emma , grandfather of Prince Henry , and the ruler of the Wish Realm . Since banishing the Evil Queen before she could cast her curse , King David rules the Wish Realm peacefully with Queen Snow White and has a child , Princess Emma , and later a grandson , Prince Henry . On the day of Prince Henry 's ascendancy to knighthood , Regina , disguised as the Evil Queen , storms into the chamber , hoping Emma would remember her past as the Savior . As she fails , Regina takes David and Snow to her palace and keeps them as hostage . In order to make Emma remember , Regina rips out David 's and Snow 's heart and crushes it .
