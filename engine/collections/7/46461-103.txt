After Kennedy 's assassination , vice president Lyndon Baines Johnson served out the remainder of the term , using appeals to finish the job that Kennedy had started to pass a remarkable package of liberal legislation that he called the Great Society . Johnson used the full powers of the presidency to ensure passage of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 . These actions helped Johnson to win a historic landslide in the 1964 presidential election over conservative champion Senator Barry Goldwater . Johnson 's big victory brought an overwhelming liberal majority in Congress .
