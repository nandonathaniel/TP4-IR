Amanda Pays portrays Tina McGee , a role she also played on the 1990 television series . A friend of Harrison Wells ( Earth - 1 version ) , she is the director of Mercury Labs and the designer of the tachyon device , which allows any object to move at the speed of light , stolen by the Reverse - Flash once during season one and again in season two . Dr. McGee is aware of Barry 's secret for some time but keeps this knowledge to herself .
