`` The Show '' was named Spin magazine 's top rap single of the year , and in Europe ( where it received air time on pop music stations such as BBC Radio 1 ) it broke the record for the best selling rap single of all time . The song peaked at # 7 on the UK Singles Chart in December 1985 and was # 8 on Jet 's top 20 for the same month . The record was produced by Dennis Bell & Ollie Cotton for City Slicker Productions .
