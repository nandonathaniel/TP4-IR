Since August 22 , 2011 , its headquarters have been in Beverly Hills , California . MGM rents space in a six - story office building . The 144,000 - square - foot ( 13,400 m ) facility was originally constructed for the venerable William Morris talent agency , but had remained all but unoccupied until MGM 's move because of the agency 's merger with Endeavor Talent Agency in April 2009 . MGM planned to house a private theater and a private outdoor patio in the building .
