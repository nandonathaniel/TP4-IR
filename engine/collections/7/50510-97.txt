On May 19 , 2002 , the series finale , `` The Truth '' , aired , and the Fox Broadcasting Company confirmed that The X-Files was not being renewed for a tenth season . When talking about the beginning of the ninth season , Chris Carter said , `` We lost our audience on the first episode . It 's like the audience had gone away , and I did n't know how to find them . I did n't want to work to get them back because I believed what we are doing deserved to have them back . '' `` The Truth '' received the highest Nielsen household rating and viewership numbers of the season . It earned a 7.5 rating and gathered 13.25 million viewers in the United States . The loss of viewers resulted in this season seeing a 30 percent ratings drop when compared to the eighth season .
