Mid-Atlantic / Upper South Maryland : a small corridor along the Youghiogheny River on the western border . West Virginia : all but the Eastern Panhandle . Kentucky : all but a small part in the extreme west drained directly by the Mississippi . Tennessee : all but a small part in the extreme west drained directly by the Mississippi , and a very small area in the southeastern corner which is drained by the Conasauga River . Virginia : most of southwest Virginia . North Carolina : the western quarter .
