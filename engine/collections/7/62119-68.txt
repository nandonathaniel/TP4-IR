On the left or right side of any Surface tablet , there is a full - size USB port , Mini DisplayPort ( or a micro-HDMI port on older models ) , and a magnetic charging port or micro-USB charging port ( Surface 3 ) . All Surface devices have a 3.5 mm headphone / microphone jack . All the devices feature an accessory spine , or Cover Port . along the bottom that has not changed in dimensions . The ports have been moved in different locations throughout the various generations of Surface tablets , and beginning with the Surface Pro 3 , Microsoft moved to a fin - style connector called Surface Connect to charge the device .
