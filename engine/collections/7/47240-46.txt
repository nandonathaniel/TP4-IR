The Three Blind Mice ( voiced by Christopher Knights and Simon J. Smith in most films , Mike Myers in Shrek in the Swamp Karaoke Dance Party ) are a trio of identical blind mice brothers with English accents that are friends with Shrek and the others . They are all blind , and that is why they sport black sunglasses and carry canes . Unlike in the fairy tale , their tails are not cut off and they show no signs of injuries . Their names are Forder , Gorder and Horder .
