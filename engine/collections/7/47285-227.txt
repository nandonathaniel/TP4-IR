The median income for a household in the city was $36,505 , and the median income for a family was $41,788 . Males had a median income of $31,106 versus $20,861 for females . The per capita income for the city was $17,022 . About 7.4 % of families and 11.6 % of the population were below the poverty line , including 14.4 % of those under age 18 and 4.4 % of those age 65 or over . A significant portion of the over 65 population is `` snow birds '' , who live in St. George during the winter months .
