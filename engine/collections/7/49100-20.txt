In September 1957 , the Monotones recorded `` The Book of Love '' , which was released on the Mascot label in December that year . The small record company could not cope with its popularity , and it was reissued on Chess Records ' subsidiary Argo label in February 1958 . On the Billboard charts , `` The Book of Love '' peaked at No. 5 on the pop chart and No. 3 on the R&B chart . Outside the US , the song reached No. 5 in Australia .
