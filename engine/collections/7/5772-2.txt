Discrimination in hiring , promotions , wages , and termination of employment and layoffs . Statements of specifications in age preference or limitations . Denial of benefits to older employees : an employer may reduce benefits based on age only if the cost of providing the reduced benefits to older workers is the same as the cost of providing full benefits to younger workers . Since 1986 , it has prohibited mandatory retirement in most sectors , with phased elimination of mandatory retirement for tenured workers , such as college professors , in 1993 .
