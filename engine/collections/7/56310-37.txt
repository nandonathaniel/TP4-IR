The Prince tries the slipper on all the women in the kingdom . When the Prince arrives at Cinderella 's home , the stepsisters try in vain to win him over . Cinderella asks if she may try , but the stepsisters taunt her . Naturally , the slipper fits perfectly , and Cinderella produces the other slipper for good measure . Cinderella 's stepfamily pleads for forgiveness , and Cinderella agrees .
