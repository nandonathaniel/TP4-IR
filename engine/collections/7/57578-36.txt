The two teams compete in a cooking challenge . Harold 's passive attitude towards hygiene prompts three campers to teach him a lesson . Meanwhile , on the other team , Leshawna and Heather 's conflict reaches a boiling point when one takes the leadership role for the challenge . The girls of a cursed team end up locking Heather in the fridge . Their carelessness in the challenge causes them to lose to the more well - organized team . In the end , Beth is voted off the island for cursing her team , and Harold learns their lesson about listening to their roommates .
