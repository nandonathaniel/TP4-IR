The McCaughey septuplets ( born November 19 , 1997 in Des Moines , Iowa ) were the world 's first surviving set of septuplets . The four boys ( Kenneth Jr. , Brandon , Nathan , and Joel ) and three girls ( Alexis , Natalie , and Kelsey ) were born at 31 weeks , weighing between 2 lbs. 5 oz. and 3 lbs 4 oz . In 2016 , they became the first set of septuplets to reach maturity .
