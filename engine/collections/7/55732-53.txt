On 23 January 2015 , the Malaysian Defence Minister Hishammuddin Hussein confirmed that Royal Malaysian Air Force ( RMAF ) is receiving S - 70A Blackhawks from the Brunei government . These helicopters , believed to be four in total , were expected to be transferred to Malaysia by September with M134D miniguns added . The four Blackhawks were delivered to Royal Brunei Air Force ( RBAF ) in 1999 are currently operated by the 4th Squadron .
