Oluo Bozado ( オルオ ・ ボザド , Oruo Bozado , alt . `` Oruo Bozad '' ) Bozado is a soldier who is introduced with 39 Titan kills and 9 assists . He is depicted as arrogant and boastful , although there is a running gag that whenever he is bragging while on his horse , he literally bites his tongue . Despite helping disable the Female Titan , he is eventually killed by one of her roundhouse kicks . He is voiced by Shinji Kawada in the Japanese anime . In the Funimation version , his name is spelled Oruo Bozad , and he is voiced by Chris Smith .
