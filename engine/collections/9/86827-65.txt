RTP released the first details regarding the opening and interval acts for the final on 12 March 2018 . The opening act of the final will feature a duet performance by Portuguese fado singers Mariza and Ana Moura , which will precede a parade of flags introducing the finalist countries , with live music by Portuguese scratching duo Beatbombers . The interval acts in the final will include Salvador Sobral , who will perform `` Amar pelos dois '' and a new single , as well as a duet with Brazilian musician Caetano Veloso , and an electronic music performance by Branko .
