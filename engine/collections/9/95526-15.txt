12 Tiruvarur Thyagarajaswamy Temple 80,937 Tiruvarur India The ancient Sri Thyagaraja temple at Tiruvarur is dedicated to the Somaskanda aspect of Shiva . The temple complex has shrines dedicated to Vanmikanathar , Tyagarajar and the Kamalaamba , and covers an area of over 20 acres ( 81,000 m ) The Kamalalayam temple tank covers around 16 acres ( 65,000 m ) , one of the largest in the country . The temple chariot is the largest of its kind in Tamil Nadu .
