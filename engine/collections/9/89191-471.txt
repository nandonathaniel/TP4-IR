In 2013 , respective bills by Rep. Schupp and Sen. Chapelle - Nadal to ban smoking statewide both failed without even being referred to a committee , while a bill by Rep. Bill Otto to prohibit localities from banning smoking in riverboat casinos underwent a committee hearing but without a vote , and a bill by Rep. Kathie Conway to deprive localities banning smoking of their sales and property tax revenue from smoking - banned private businesses was passed by the House Local Government Committee , but did not advance to a vote before the full House of Representatives .
