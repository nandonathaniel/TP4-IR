Mjolnir is briefly seen by itself during a post-credits scene in Iron Man 2 ( 2010 ) at the bottom of a giant crater in New Mexico ; S.H.I.E.L.D. Agent Phil Coulson informs Nick Fury , `` We 've found it . '' It has since appeared in every Marvel Cinematic Universe film that features Thor including Thor ( 2011 ) , The Avengers ( 2012 ) , Thor : The Dark World ( 2013 ) , and Avengers : Age of Ultron ( 2015 ) . In Thor : Ragnarok ( 2017 ) , Hela destroys Mjolnir during a battle with Thor , but at the film 's conclusion Thor learns how to channel his power without using the hammer as a focus .
