Beagles have been featured across a wide range of media . References to the dog appear before the 19th century in works by such writers as William Shakespeare , John Webster , John Dryden , Thomas Tickell , Henry Fielding , and William Cowper , as well as in Alexander Pope 's translation of Homer 's Iliad . Beagles appeared in funny animal comic strips and animated cartoons from the 1950s with Courage the Cowardly Dog and the Peanuts character Snoopy billed as `` the world 's most famous Beagle '' .
