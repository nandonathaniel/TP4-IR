The Cars 2 soundtrack was released on both CD album and digital download June 14 . It is the fourth Pixar film to be scored by Michael Giacchino after The Incredibles , Ratatouille and Up . It also marks the first time that Giacchino has worked with John Lasseter as a director , as Lasseter had been executive producer on Giacchino 's previous three Pixar films and that Lasseter has not worked with Randy Newman .
