As of January 2018 , Boeing and Goldman Sachs are among the highest priced stocks in the average and therefore have the greatest influence on it . Alternately , Pfizer and General Electric are among the lowest priced stocks in the average and have the least amount of sway in the price movement . Many critics of the DJIA recommend the float - adjusted market - value weighted S&P 500 or the Wilshire 5000 , the latter of which includes all U.S. equity securities , as better indicators of the U.S. stock market .
