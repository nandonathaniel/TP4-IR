A ballet by Christopher Wheeldon and Nicholas Wright commissioned for The Royal Ballet , titled Alice 's Adventures in Wonderland , premiered in February 2011 at the Royal Opera House in Covent Garden , London . The ballet was based on the novel Wheeldon grew up reading as a child and is generally faithful to the original story , although some critics claimed it may have been too faithful . The ballet overall stays generally light hearted for its running time of an hour and 40 minutes . The ballet returned to the Royal Opera House in 2012 .
