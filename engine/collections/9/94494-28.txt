The Master , played by Roger Delgado , makes his first appearance in Terror of the Autons ( 1971 ) , where he allies with the Nestene Consciousness to help them invade Earth . The Third Doctor ( Jon Pertwee ) convinces the Master to stop this plan at the last minute , and the Master subsequently escapes , albeit with his TARDIS , a space - time ship , left non-functioning after the Doctor confiscates the ship 's dematerialisation circuit .
