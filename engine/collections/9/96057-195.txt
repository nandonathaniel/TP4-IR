Reaction forces : A modified 16 Air Assault Brigade and an armoured division ( 3rd Division ) of three armoured infantry brigades ( 1st , 12th and 20th Armoured Infantry Brigades ) . In 2020 , this division will reorganise and consist of two armoured infantry brigades and two strike brigades . Adaptive forces : The 1st Division , consisting of seven infantry brigades . In 2020 , the adaptive force will consist of specialised infantry battalions who will train , advise , assist , mentor and accompany operations by indigenous forces . Force Troops Command : Nine brigades of supporting units which supplement the reaction and adaptive forces .
