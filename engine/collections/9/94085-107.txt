The Battle of Maldon ( 312 - 319 ) Modern English West Saxon Thought shall be the harder , the heart the keener , courage the greater , as our strength lessens . Here lies our leader in the dust , all cut down ; always may he mourn who now thinks to turn away from this warplay . I am old , I will not go away , but I plan to lie down by the side of my lord , by the man so dearly loved .
