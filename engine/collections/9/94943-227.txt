On January 3 , 2018 , President Donald Trump nominated Josephine `` Jody '' Olson as the 20th director of the Peace Corps. . Olson has a long history with the agency , serving as Acting Director in 2009 , Deputy Director from 2002 - 2009 , Chief of Staff from 1989 - 1992 , Regional Director , North Africa Near East , Asia , Pacific from 1981 - 1984 , and Country Director in Togo from 1979 - 1981 . Olsen also served as a Peace Corps volunteer in Tunisia from 1966 - 1968 .
