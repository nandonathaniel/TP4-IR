`` Shape of You '' peaked at number - one on the singles charts of 44 countries , including the US Billboard Hot 100 , as well as the UK , Australian and Canadian singles charts . It has stayed at number one for 16 consecutive weeks on the Canadian Hot 100 tied for the record , and 14 non-consecutive weeks on the UK Singles Chart , and 12 non-consecutive weeks on the Billboard Hot 100 . On September 21 , 2017 , it became the most streamed song on Spotify , with over 1.32 billion streams .
