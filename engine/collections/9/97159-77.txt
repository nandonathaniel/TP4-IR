Chennai city is governed by the Greater Chennai Corporation ( formerly `` Corporation of Madras '' ) , which was established in 1688 . It is the oldest surviving municipal corporation in India and the second oldest surviving corporation in the world . In 2011 , the jurisdiction of the Chennai Corporation was expanded from 174 km ( 67 sq mi ) to an area of 426 km ( 164 sq mi ) , dividing into three regions -- North , South and Central , which covers 200 wards . The corporation is headed by a mayor , an office presently occupied by Saidai Sa . Duraisamy . The Mayor and councillors of the city are elected through a popular vote by the residents . While the city limit was expanded in 2011 , the revised population is yet to be officially announced .
