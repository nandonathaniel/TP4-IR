Since the Islanders moved to Barclays Center , there have been numerous complaints about obstructed view seats , due to the fact that the arena was not built with ice hockey in mind . Business Insider has called sections 201 to 204 and 228 to 231 , `` the worst seat in American professional sports '' . In an interview with Sports Illustrated , Barclays Center CEO Brett Yormark acknowledged the issue , but insisted nothing can be done : `` There 's really nothing we 're going to do from a capital improvement standpoint . You can watch the game on your mobile device . The game is on the scoreboard . ''
