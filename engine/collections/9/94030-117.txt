As of the census of 2000 , there were 29,183 people , 4,315 households , and 4,215 families residing on the base . The population density was 1,540.0 people per square mile ( 594.6 / km2 ) . There were 4,420 housing units at an average density of 233.3 / sq mi ( 90.1 / km2 ) . Fort Bragg was not recorded as a census - designated place for the 2010 census .
