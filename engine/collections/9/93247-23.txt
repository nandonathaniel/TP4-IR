George Lindsey was born in Fairfield , Alabama and grew up in the small town of Jasper . He graduated from Walker County High School ( Jasper ) in 1946 and then attended Kemper Military School in Boonville , Missouri , and Florence State Teacher 's College ( Florence , Alabama ) ( now the University of North Alabama ) , where he majored in physical education and biology , played quarterback on the football team , and received a Bachelor of Science in 1952 .
