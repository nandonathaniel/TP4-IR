Admiral Ozawa had transferred to the destroyer Wakatsuki after Taihō had been hit , but the radio gear on board was incapable of sending the number of messages needed , so he transferred again , to the carrier Zuikaku , at 13 : 00 . It was then he learned of the disastrous results of the previous day and that he had about 150 aircraft left . Nevertheless , he decided to continue the attacks , thinking there were still hundreds of aircraft on Guam and Rota , and started planning new raids for June 21 .
