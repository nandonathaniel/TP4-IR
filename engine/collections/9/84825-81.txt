An original cast recording released as a six - disc 78 rpm set by Columbia Records was the label 's first recording of a Broadway musical . The label used the album to introduce its new LP format in June 1948 . In 1988 , the album was released on CD , and in 2000 , a second CD version appeared that was remastered from the original acetates and restored some material originally recorded but cut from the show , including three bonus tracks in which Harburg discusses the writing of and sings `` How Are Things in Glocca Morra ? '' and `` When I 'm Not Near the Girl I Love , '' as well as singing `` Do n't Pass Me By , '' a song cut from the show .
