Phyllis was introduced as a groupie of fictional rock singer Danny Romalotti ( Michael Damian ) , and claimed to have given birth to his child , Daniel ( Michael Graziadei ) . Danny married Phyllis out of obligation to his son , sparking a rivalry between Phyllis and Danny 's ex-wife , Christine Blair ( Lauralee Bell ) , which is a continuing storyline . The character is known for scheming to get what she wants . Phyllis has also been involved with Jack Abbott ( Peter Bergman ) and Nicholas Newman ( Joshua Morrow ) ; both relationships have fan followings .
