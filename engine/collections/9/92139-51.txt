1249 - 1251 Andrew of Longjumeau guide a French ambassador to the great Kuyuk Khan . His brother Guy and several others -- John Goderiche , John of Carcassonne , Herbert `` Le Sommelier '' , Gerbert of Sens , Robert ( a clerk ) , a certain William , and an unnamed clerk of Poissy go with him . They reached Talas in northwestern Kyrgyzstan .
