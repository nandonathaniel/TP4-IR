On December 19 , 2015 , Paris and other worldwide fans commemorated the 100th anniversary of the birth of Edith Piaf -- a French cabaret singer , songwriter and actress who became widely regarded as France 's national chanteuse , as well as being one of France 's greatest international stars . Other singers -- of similar style -- include Maurice Chevalier , Charles Aznavour , Yves Montand , and Charles Trenet .
