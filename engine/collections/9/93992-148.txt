A merchant who was first seen trying to flee with his packages intact when the Titans breached Wall Rose . However , a menacing threat from Mikasa forced Reeves to move his cart from the tunnel to let the villagers escape to the safety of Wall Sheena . He appears later when his group tries to kidnap Eren and Krista , but is foiled by the Survey Corps . He agrees to Levi 's terms to support the Survey Corps in their rebellion against the King . However , Dimo is killed by Captain Ackerman when he sees through their plan and realize that he was working with them .
