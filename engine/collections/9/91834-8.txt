Born in Jersey City , New Jersey , of Italian ancestry , Micucci was raised in Nazareth , Pennsylvania , in the Lehigh Valley region of the state , where she first learned to play classical piano , taught by her mother . She graduated in 1998 from Nazareth Area High School . Micucci then received an A.A. in Fine Arts from Keystone College in La Plume , Pennsylvania , and a B.A. in Studio Art in 2003 from Loyola Marymount University , Los Angeles .
