For the next 15 years , Michael ( Tyler Mane ) continues making masks and not speaking to anyone . Loomis , having continued to treat Michael over the years , attempts to move on with his life and closes Michael 's case . Later , Michael kills the sanitarium employees and escapes from Smith 's Grove . He kills a truck driver for his clothes , and makes his way back to Haddonfield . On Halloween , Michael arrives at his now - abandoned childhood home , where he recovers the kitchen knife and Halloween mask he stored under the floorboards the night he killed his sister .
