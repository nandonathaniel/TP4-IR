Trinity Church held the title of tallest building in the United States until 1869 , when it was surpassed by St. Michael 's Church , Old Town , Chicago . At the time of its completion , Trinity 's 281 - foot ( 86 m ) spire and cross comprised the highest point in New York , until it was surpassed in 1883 by the stone tower of the Brooklyn Bridge , and in 1890 by the New York World Building .
