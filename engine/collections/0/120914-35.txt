In 2007 , an eclectic panel of renowned recording artists voted `` Tutti Frutti '' number one on Mojo 's The Top 100 Records That Changed The World , hailing the recording as `` the sound of the birth of rock and roll . '' The song is # 43 in Rolling Stone 's list of The 500 Greatest Songs of All Time . In April 2012 , Rolling Stone magazine declared that the song `` still has the most inspired rock lyric on record . ''
