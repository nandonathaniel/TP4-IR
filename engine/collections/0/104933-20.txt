Wyandotte County ( / ˈwaɪ. ən dɒt / ; county code WY ) is a county located in the U.S. state of Kansas . As of the 2010 census , the population was 157,505 , making it the fourth-most populous county in Kansas . Its county seat and most populous city is Kansas City , with which it shares a unified government . Wyandotte County lies immediately west of Kansas City , Missouri .
