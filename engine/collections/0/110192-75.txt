On January 31 , 2013 , ORU announced that William `` Billy '' Wilson , executive director of the International Center for Spiritual Renewal and vice-chair of the ORU board of trustees , had been selected to succeed Rutland as president , effective on July 1 , 2013 . A day earlier , the ORU student newspaper had briefly posted an erroneous report on its website , mistakenly identifying a different person as the new president . Rutland criticized the report at a student assembly and the newspaper 's longtime faculty adviser left the school the next day .
