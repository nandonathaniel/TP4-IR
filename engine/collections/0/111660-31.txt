Injustice 2 is a fighting video game developed by NetherRealm Studios and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment . It is the sequel to 2013 's Injustice : Gods Among Us . The game was initially released in May 2017 for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One ; a Microsoft Windows version was released later in November 2017 . An expanded version of the game , titled Injustice 2 : Legendary Edition , was released in March 2018 for the PlayStation 4 , Xbox One , and Microsoft Windows . Similar to the previous installment , a companion mobile app was released for Android and iOS devices . A prequel comic book series of the same name , written by Tom Taylor , was also released beginning in April 2017 .
