Ginny is a very talented and powerful witch . At the age of fourteen she was able to conjure a corporeal Patronus Charm -- a very advanced piece of magic and proof that she possessed superior magical ability . According to Pottermore , Ginny 's patronus is a horse . She is also a gifted flier and Quidditch player , in particular having scored `` seventeen goals '' during a Quidditch practice . Ginny became known to use very adeptly the Bat - Bogey Hex , most notably on Draco Malfoy and Zacharias Smith .
