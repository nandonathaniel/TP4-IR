After using their bionics to save Leo from a falling air conditioning unit , a suspicious Principal Perry catches the Lab Rats on video using their bionics , but she has not viewed the footage yet . In order to stop her from showing Donald and Tasha the film on parent - teacher night , Leo uses Donald 's invisibility cloak to sneak into her office . However , when Leo gets caught , the Lab Rats must think of a new plan . Guest stars : Angel Parker as Tasha , Maile Flanagan as Principal Perry
