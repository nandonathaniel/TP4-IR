Olivia Holt as Tandy Bowen / Dagger : A teenager and street thief who is connected to Tyrone Johnson through a shared childhood tragedy , with the ability to emit light daggers . Showrunner Joe Pokaski felt Bowen in the series was `` more cynical '' than previous portrayals , and noted that when she touches people she can access their hopes and desires , saying , `` There 's something exciting about having a cynical character like Tandy who steals things and does n't believe in the good of man , yet sees the best in them when she touches them '' . Rachel Ryals portrays a young Tandy .
