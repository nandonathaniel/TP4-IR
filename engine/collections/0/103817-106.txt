On November 9 , 2010 , Hasbro Studios has announced a broadcasting rights deal with Corus Entertainment in Canada , which includes Transformers : Prime . The series premiered there on January 9 , 2011 on Teletoon , half - owned by Corus . As a part of the deal between Hasbro Studios and Turner Broadcasting System Europe announced on December 13 , 2010 , Transformers : Prime started broadcasting in the United Kingdom and Ireland on September 5 , 2011 on Cartoon Network , and later on Cartoon Network Too , and in Central Europe , South Africa , and the Middle East on September 10 , 2011 on the pan-European Cartoon Network .
