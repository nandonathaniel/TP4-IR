Animation pioneer Winsor McCay spent nearly two years animating the disaster for his film The Sinking of the Lusitania ( 1918 ) . At 12 minutes , it was the longest animated film on record at the time . it was also the earliest - known dramatic animation . The docudrama Sinking of the Lusitania : Terror at Sea ( 2007 ) depicts the last voyage of the Lusitania and the political and military decisions that led to the sinking .
