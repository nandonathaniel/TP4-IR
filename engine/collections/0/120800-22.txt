As of 2001 India census , Devprayag had a population of 2144 . Males constitute 52 % of the population and females 48 % . Devprayag has an average literacy rate of 77 % , higher than the national average of 74.5 % ; male literacy is 82 % and , female literacy is 72 % . In Devprayag , 13 % of the population is under 6 years of age . The town is the seat of the pandas of the Badrinath Dham .
