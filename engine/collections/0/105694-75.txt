When aired in syndication , the series is edited from its original HBO broadcast ( for running time and without the TV - MA scenes ) . On June 2 , 2010 , the series premiered on the TV Guide Network , making its basic cable debut . The network also recorded a series of related discussions with high - profile guest stars , media pundits , and prominent social figures called `` Curb : The Discussion '' debating the moral implications depicted in each episode . The show was also syndicated on local stations and WGN America , but has been removed from syndication from both outlets owing to low ratings . It debuted on TV Land in February 2013 .
