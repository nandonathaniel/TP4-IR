The same bill was introduced into the United States Senate as S. 358 by Senators Olympia Snowe , Ted Kennedy , Mike Enzi , and Christopher Dodd . On 2008 - 04 - 24 , the Senate approved the bill 95 - 0 , with five Senators not voting ( including presidential candidates McCain , Clinton , and Obama ) . It had been subject of a `` hold '' placed by Tom Coburn , M.D. , Republican U.S. Senator from Oklahoma .
