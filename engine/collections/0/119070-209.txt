One of Columbia 's greatest assets is Riverbanks Zoo & Garden . Riverbanks Zoo is a sanctuary for more than 2,000 animals housed in natural habitat exhibits along the Saluda River . Just across the river , the 70 - acre ( 280,000 m ) botanical garden is devoted to gardens , woodlands , plant collections , and historic ruins . Riverbanks has been named one of America 's best zoos and the No. 1 travel attraction in the Southeast . It attracted over one million visitors in 2009 .
