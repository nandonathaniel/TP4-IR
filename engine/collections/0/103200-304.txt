Dana can not determine what to do in college and J.T. and Rich are not able to major in `` Nude Volleyball , '' so they all take a career - placement test from a clueless proctor ( Billy Stevenson ) . The results of the placement test say J.T. and Rich should become doctors and Dana should become a guard at a women 's prison . Livid with these results , Dana demands a retest and discovers that the test center run by Mr. Newton ( Rick Scarry ) is a fraud . Also , Frank hires a hyperactive new worker named Flash who has never worked in construction before .
