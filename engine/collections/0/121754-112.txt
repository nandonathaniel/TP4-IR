A painting which dates from around 1810 , and has been on display at the International Bowling Hall of Fame and Museum in St. Louis , Missouri ( before its relocation on January 26 , 2010 , to the International Bowling Campus in Arlington , Texas ) , shows British bowlers playing the sport outdoors in the earliest known pictorial depiction of `` ten - pin bowling '' of any type , with a triangular formation of ten pins , chronologically before it appeared in the United States . A photograph of this painting appeared in the pages of the US - based `` Bowler 's Journal '' magazine in 1988 .
