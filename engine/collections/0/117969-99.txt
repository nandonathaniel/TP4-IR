The Martian was a financial success . It grossed $228.4 million in the United States & Canada and $401.7 million in other countries , for a worldwide total of $630.2 million against a budget of $108 million . Worldwide , it was the highest - grossing Fox film of 2015 and the tenth - highest - grossing film of that year overall . Deadline Hollywood calculated the net profit of the film to be $150.32 million while The Hollywood Reporter reported around $80 -- 100 million profits for the film .
