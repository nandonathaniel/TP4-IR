As Ben and Gwen are planning Max 's birthday , a mysterious stranger appears out of nowhere and kidnaps her , so Ben follows her into a strange portal as XLR8 . When the dust settles , Ben suddenly realizes he is twenty years later in the future , where humans and aliens live peacefully . Ben then learns that Gwen 's captor is her own thirty - year - old future self , and a master sorceress to boot . Gwendolyn reveals that the current Ben , called Ben 10,000 , is now a world - renowned hero with ten thousand different alien forms at his disposal . But , upon meeting his future self , Ben realizes that the future Ben is all business and just ca n't relax and have fun , not even for his own family .
