Grand Theft Auto : Vice City is an action - adventure third - person shooter video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games . It was released on 27 October 2002 for the PlayStation 2 , on 12 May 2003 for Microsoft Windows , and on 31 October 2003 for the Xbox . A remastered version was released for mobile platforms in 2012 , for the game 's tenth anniversary . It is the sixth title in the Grand Theft Auto series and the first main entry since 2001 's Grand Theft Auto III . Set within the fictional Vice City , based on Miami , the game follows Tommy Vercetti following his release from prison . After he is caught up in an ambushed drug deal , he seeks out those responsible while building a criminal empire and seizing power from other criminal organisations in the city .
