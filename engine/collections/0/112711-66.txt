The episode was written by the showrunners David Benioff and D.B. Weiss . Like the rest of the first season , it adapts the plot of A Game of Thrones , the first novel in the A Song of Ice and Fire series by George R.R. Martin . The episode covers the novel 's chapters 66 to 73 , that is , Arya V , Bran VII , Sansa VI , Daenerys IX , Tyrion IX , Jon IX , Catelyn XI and Daenerys X . It also covers part of the second novel , A Clash of Kings : Arya I ( chapter 2 ) and part of Catelyn VII ( chapter 55 ) . Scenes added for the adaptation include Catelyn and Robb receiving news of Eddard 's death , the revelation of Cersei and Lancel Lannister 's relationship , as well as interactions between Grand Maester Pycelle , the prostitute Ros , Varys and Littlefinger .
