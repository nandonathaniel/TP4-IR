The Battle of the Labyrinth was first published in the United States by Hyperion Books for Children , an imprint of Disney Publishing Worldwide . Since its release , The Battle of the Labyrinth has been translated into Bulgarian , Catalan ( Valencian ) , Chinese , Czech , Danish , Dutch , Estonian , Finnish , French , German , Greek , Hebrew , Hungarian , Indonesian , Italian , Lithuanian , Norwegian , Persian , Polish , Portuguese , Romanian , Russian , Slovak , Slovenian , Spanish , Swedish , Thai , Turkish and Vietnamese .
