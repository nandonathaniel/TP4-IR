Count Kirill Vladimirovich Bezukhov , father of Pierre Count Pyotr Kirillovich ( `` Pierre '' Bezukhov ) : The central character and often a voice for Tolstoy 's own beliefs or struggles . Pierre is the socially awkward illegitimate son of Count Kirill Vladimirovich Bezukhov , who has fathered dozens of illegitimate sons . Educated abroad , Pierre returns to Russia as a misfit . His unexpected inheritance of a large fortune makes him socially desirable .
