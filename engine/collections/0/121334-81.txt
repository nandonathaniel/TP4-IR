Blue Cross and Blue Shield insurance companies are licensees , independent of the association and traditionally of each other , offering insurance plans within defined regions under one or both of the association 's brands . Blue Cross Blue Shield insurers offer some form of health insurance coverage in every U.S. state . They also act as administrators of Medicare in many states or regions of the U.S. and provide coverage to state government employees as well as to the federal government employees under a nationwide option of the Federal Employees Health Benefits Program .
