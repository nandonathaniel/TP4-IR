On May 10 , 2015 , Last Man Standing was renewed for a fifth season . At the start of this season , a different cast member narrating that the series is recorded in front of a live studio audience at the start of each episode is discontinued . Jay Leno guest starred as Joe Leonard , a mechanic in the episodes `` The Road Less Driven '' , `` Mike and the Mechanics '' and the season finale `` The Shortcut '' . Patricia Richardson reprised her role as Helen Potts in the episode `` Tanks for the Memories '' . Bill Engvall guest starred in the episode `` The Marriage Doctor '' , reuniting with his Bill Engvall Show co-star Nancy Travis .
