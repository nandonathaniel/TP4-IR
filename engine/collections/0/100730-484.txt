Cloud - based supplementary features Incus ( species capillatus only ) Cumulonimbus with flat anvil - like cirriform top caused by wind shear where the rising air currents hit the inversion layer at the tropopause . Mamma Also sometimes called Mammatus , consisting of bubble - like protrusions on the underside caused by localized downdrafts . Arcus ( including roll and shelf clouds ) Low , horizontal cloud formation associated with the leading edge of thunderstorm outflow . Tuba Column hanging from the cloud base which can develop into a funnel cloud or tornado .
