Poland 15 - 18 * 15 2002 After graduating from an 8 - year primary school ( usually at the age of 14 or 15 ) one can leave school but is legally required to continue education up to the age of 18 . However , this requirement can be satisfied through vocational training , without attending a school . This can be done as a part of employment , so technically the person leaves a school and works full - time .
