According to the Raspberry Pi Foundation , more than 5 million Raspberry Pis were sold by February 2015 , making it the best - selling British computer . By November 2016 they had sold 11 million units , and 12.5 m by March 2017 , making it the third best - selling `` general purpose computer '' . In July 2017 , sales reached nearly 15 million . In March 2018 , sales reached 19 million .
