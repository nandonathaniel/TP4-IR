Navadurga ( Sanskrit : नवदुर्गा , lit . Nine forms of Durga ) , are nine manifestations of the Goddess Durga in Hinduism , especially worshipped during the festival of Navratri where each of the nine manifested forms are consecutively venerated throughout all the nine nights . The nine manifested forms of Goddess Durga are : Shailaputri , Brahmacharini , Chandraghanta , Kushmanda , Skandamata , Katyayini , Kaalratri , Mahagauri and Siddhidhatri .
