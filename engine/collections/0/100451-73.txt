In early 2011 , Djokovic became the primary challenge for Nadal after beating him in Indian Wells and Miami back - to - back . As the clay season began , Djokovic 's winning streak continued as he beat Nadal for the first time on clay in Madrid , ending Nadal 's second streak of 37 straight wins on clay . As such , Djokovic became the second man in tennis history after Roger Federer to ever defeat Rafael Nadal in a clay final . Djokovic beat Nadal again a week later in Rome . Djokovic was a legitimate contender going into the 2011 French Open and was perceived by many tennis analysts as capable of dethroning Nadal . Djokovic and Federer met in the semifinals , where Federer 's win put an end to Djokovic 's best opportunity to win the French Open . Djokovic had been on a 43 - match winning streak , including four straight wins over Rafael Nadal up to that point . Federer 's win also prevented Djokovic from winning the Calendar Grand Slam .
