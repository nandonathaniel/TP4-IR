In May 2017 , a survey conducted by United Kingdom 's Royal Society for Public Health , featuring 1,479 people aged 14 -- 24 , asking them to rate social media platforms depending on anxiety , depression , loneliness , bullying and body image , concluded that Instagram was `` worst for young mental health '' . In response , Instagram stated that `` Keeping Instagram a safe and supportive place for young people was a top priority '' .
