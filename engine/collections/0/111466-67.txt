John Allen Muhammad ( 1960 -- 2009 ) and Lee Boyd Malvo ( born 1975 ) , perpetrators of the Beltway sniper attacks , a series of coordinated shootings that took place over three weeks in October 2002 in Maryland , Virginia , and Washington , D.C. Ten people were killed and three other victims were critically injured in several locations throughout the Washington , D.C. metropolitan area and along Interstate 95 in Virginia .
