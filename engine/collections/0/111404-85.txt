Regarded as pioneers of live music performance and renowned for their lavish stage shows , Pink Floyd also set high standards in sound quality , making use of innovative sound effects and quadraphonic speaker systems . From their earliest days , they employed visual effects to accompany their psychedelic rock music while performing at venues such as the UFO Club in London . Their slide - and - light show was one of the first in British rock , and it helped them become popular among London 's underground .
