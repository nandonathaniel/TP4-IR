On November 12 , 2014 , Mark Gordon 's The Mark Gordon Company bought the feature film adaptation rights to Molly Bloom 's memoir , Molly 's Game : The True Story of the 26 - Year - Old Woman Behind the Most Exclusive , High - Stakes Underground Poker Game in the World , which Gordon produced . Aaron Sorkin was hired to adapt the memoir into a screenplay . Bloom had already approached Sorkin , as he was her `` favorite writer '' . On January 7 , 2016 , it was announced that Sorkin would make his directorial debut on the film , for Sony Pictures Entertainment , while Amy Pascal also produced . On February 18 , 2016 , Sony exited the project , and on May 13 , 2016 , STX Entertainment came on board , and subsequently bought the film 's US and Chinese distribution rights for $9 million .
