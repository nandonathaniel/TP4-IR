In 1994 , Pall Mall and Lucky Strike were purchased by Brown & Williamson Tobacco Corporation as the former American Tobacco company shed its tobacco brands . In 2001 , Pall Mall was re-branded as a savings brand , and introduced several varieties of filtered cigarettes . Brown & Williamson merged with R.J. Reynolds Tobacco Company on July 30 , 2004 , with the surviving company taking the name , R.J. Reynolds Tobacco Company . R.J. Reynolds continues to make unfiltered and filtered styles of Pall Mall for the U.S. market , emphasizing the latter . British American Tobacco makes and sells Pall Mall outside the U.S.
