The soundtrack album contains original score composed and produced by John Debney , with a music by a wide range of artists , some musical veterans , such as Patti LaBelle and Diana Ross , as well as others . Uniquely for a Disney animated film , several of the songs are covers of classic popular songs , such as Elton John and Kiki Dee 's `` Do n't Go Breaking My Heart '' , Carole King 's `` It 's Too Late '' , and the Spice Girls ' signature hit `` Wannabe '' . The soundtrack was released on November 1 , 2005 , by Walt Disney Records .
