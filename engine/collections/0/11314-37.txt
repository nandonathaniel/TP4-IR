The Madras Presidency annexed the kingdom of Mysore in 1831 on allegations of maladministration and restored it to Chamaraja Wodeyar ( 1881 -- 94 ) , the grandson and heir of the deposed Mummadi Krishnaraja Wodeyar ( 1799 -- 1868 ) in 1881 . Thanjavur was annexed in 1855 , following the death of Shivaji II ( 1832 -- 1855 ) who left no male heir .
