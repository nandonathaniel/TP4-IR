Salicylic acid has the formula C H ( OH ) COOH , where the OH group is ortho to the carboxyl group . It is also known as 2 - hydroxybenzoic acid . It is poorly soluble in water ( 2 g / L at 20 Â° C ) . Aspirin ( acetylsalicylic acid or ASA ) can be prepared by the esterification of the phenolic hydroxyl group of salicylic acid with the acetyl group from acetic anhydride or acetyl chloride . Salicylic acid can also be prepared using the Kolbe - Schmitt reaction .
