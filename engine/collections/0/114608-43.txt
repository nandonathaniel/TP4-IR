Studio 2 - 7,271 sq ft ( 675.5 m ) - The second largest on the complex houses shows such as Piers Morgan 's Life Stories , The Alan Titchmarsh Show , Let 's Do Lunch with Gino & Mel , Have I Got News for You and It 'll Be Alright on the Night . The studio can accommodate large audiences of up to 462 . The studio can also be used as a bare shell as the audience seating structure can be moved out . Studio 2 was upgraded to HD in the summer of 2009 .
