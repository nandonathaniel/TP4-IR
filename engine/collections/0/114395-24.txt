When Robin heads to the roof of the building , she sees no sign of Patrice . Instead she finds a page from Barney 's Playbook , titled `` The Robin '' . Barney had a long plan to get back together with Robin , which started with proclaiming his love for Robin and intentionally getting shot down . He went to Patrice for help and pretended to date her so that Robin would realize her feelings for him . When Barney arrives , Robin feels that she ca n't trust Barney because of how he manipulated her to get to this moment . He still proclaims his love for her and proposes ; Robin accepts , and they embrace and kiss .
