Shane Warne , regarded as one of the best bowlers in the history of the game , holds several Test records . He held the record for the most Test wickets with 708 until December 2007 when Sri Lankan bowler Muttiah Muralitharan passed Warne 's milestone . Warne is second only to Muralitharan in taking the most five - wicket hauls in an innings and the most ten - wicket hauls in a Test match . Glenn McGrath , who took 563 wickets in his career , holds the record for the most wickets taken by a fast bowler in Test cricket . Adam Gilchrist is Australia 's most successful wicket - keeper having taken 416 dismissals . He is second only to South Africa 's Mark Boucher with 555 to his name . Allan Border , who made his Test debut in 1978 and captained Australia from 1984 until his retirement in 1994 , holds the Test record for the most consecutive matches played with 153 and the record for the most matches played as skipper for Australia with 93 .
