In 1990 , the park was purchased by The Tussauds Group . The change of ownership heralded an era of intense development , with a range of large attractions and new areas being built at the park , including the dark rides The Haunted House ( 1992 ) and Toyland Tours ( 1994 ) , thrill rides such as Nemesis ( 1994 ) , Oblivion ( 1998 ) and Air / Galactica ( 2002 ) , and a hotel complex in 1996 .
