Zoo Administrator ( Fred Willard ) of the zoo Ross sent Marcel to after Marcel started humping everything . When Marcel is kidnapped and forced into show business , he and the rest of the zoo staff decide to cover up the break - in out of fear of bad publicity , and when Ross enters the zoo with the intention of paying Marcel a visit , he lies and informs Ross that Marcel has died . Ross later learns the truth about what happened to Marcel from the Zoo Janitor ( Dan Castellaneta ) , a very unusual man with a strange fascination for the animals in the zoo .
