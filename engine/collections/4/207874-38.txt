According to the United States Census Bureau , the city has a total area of 5.8 square miles ( 15 km ) , of which 4.9 square miles ( 13 km ) is land and 0.8 square miles ( 2.1 km ) ( 14.58 % ) is water . Overlooking lakes Dora , Gertrude and Beauclair , Mount Dora is situated on a plateau 184 feet ( 56 m ) above sea level . Despite the name , there is no mountain nearby .
