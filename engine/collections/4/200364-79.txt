Presenting Pelé a lifetime achievement award , former South African president Nelson Mandela said ; `` To watch him play was to watch the delight of a child combined with the extraordinary grace of a man in full . '' US politician and political scientist Henry Kissinger stated , `` Performance at a high level in any sport is to exceed the ordinary human scale . But Pelé 's performance transcended that of the ordinary star by as much as the star exceeds ordinary performance . '' After a reporter asked if his fame compared to that of Jesus , Pelé in response quipped , `` There are parts of the world where Jesus Christ is not so well known . ''
