The city has various museums including the Ho Chi Minh City Museum , Museum of Vietnamese History , the Revolutionary Museum , the Museum of south - eastern Armed Forces , the War Remnants Museum , the Museum of Southern Women , the Museum of Fine Arts , the Nha Rong Memorial House , and the Ben Duoc Relic of Underground Tunnels . The Củ Chi tunnels are north - west of the city in Củ Chi District . The Saigon Zoo and Botanical Gardens , in District 1 , dates from 1865 . The Đầm Sen Tourist and Cultural Park , Suối Tiên Amusement and Culture Park , and Cần Giờ 's Eco beach resort are three recreational sites inside the city which are popular with tourists .
