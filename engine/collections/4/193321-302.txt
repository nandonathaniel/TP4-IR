Both the UPI and AP panels ranked Delaware ( 10 -- 0 ) first , followed by Louisiana Tech ( 11 -- 0 ) , and Cal Poly SLO ( 8 -- 0 -- 1 ) . Louisiana Tech later defeated Tennessee Tech in the Grantland Rice Bowl , while Cal Poly SLO lost to North Dakota in the Camellia Bowl . Delaware declined an invitation to face Massachusetts ( 7 -- 2 ) in the Boardwalk Bowl , and did not play in the postseason .
