Dichlorine monoxide ( Cl O ) is a brownish - yellow gas ( red - brown when solid or liquid ) which may be obtained by reacting chlorine gas with yellow mercury ( II ) oxide . It is very soluble in water , in which it is in equilibrium with hypochlorous acid ( HOCl ) , which it is the anhydride of . It is thus an effective bleach and is mostly used to make hypochlorites . It explodes on heating or sparking or in the presence of ammonia gas .
