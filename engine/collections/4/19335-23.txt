Caine Lawson and his best friend Kevin `` O - Dog '' Anderson enter a local store to buy malt liquor , where the suspicious Korean storekeeper and his wife urge them to quickly purchase their drinks and leave . As they do , the storekeeper angers O - Dog by remarking about feeling sorry for his mother . A brief argument ends with the couple being murdered by O - Dog , who takes the store 's surveillance tape , robs the dead storekeeper 's wallet , and empties the cash register . In a flashback , it is revealed that Caine 's father was a drug dealer who was killed when he was 10 , that his mother was a heroin addict who later died of a drug overdose , and that he was raised by his grandparents .
