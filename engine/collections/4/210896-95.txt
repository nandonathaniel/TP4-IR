The total cost of the monument from 1848 to 1888 was $1,409,500 ( equivalent to $30,000,000 in 2016 ) . The weight of the above ground portion of the monument is 44,208 long tons ( 49,513 short tons ; 44,917 tonnes ) , whereas its total weight , including the foundation below ground and any earth above it that is within its outer perimeter is 81,120 long tons ( 90,854 short tons ; 82,422 tonnes ) . The total number of blocks in the monument , including all marble , granite and gneiss blocks , whether externally or internally visible or hidden from view within the walls or old foundation is over 36,000 . The number of marble blocks externally visible is about 10,000 .
