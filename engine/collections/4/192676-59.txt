In 1987 , a video was released to MTV and other channels , featuring a live performance of `` Born to Run '' from Springsteen and the E Street Band 's 1984 - 1985 Born in the U.S.A. Tour , interspersed with clips of other songs ' performances from the same tour . It closed with a `` Thank you '' message to Springsteen 's fans . In 1988 , director Meiert Avis shot a video of an acoustic version of the song during the Tunnel of Love Express tour .
