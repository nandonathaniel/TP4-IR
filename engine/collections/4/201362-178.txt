Best Short Subject ( Cartoon ) Walt Disney won eight consecutive awards for Flowers and Trees in 1931 -- 32 , Three Little Pigs in 1932 -- 33 , The Tortoise and the Hare in 1934 , Three Orphan Kittens in 1935 , The Country Cousin in 1936 , The Old Mill in 1937 , Ferdinand the Bull in 1938 , and The Ugly Duckling in 1939
