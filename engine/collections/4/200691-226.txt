2 October Second of two Short SC. 1 VTOL experimental testbeds , XG905 , a compact tailless delta monoplane with five Rolls - Royce RB108 engines , one for propulsion and four for lift , crashes while attempting landing at Belfast , Northern Ireland . Gyros failed , producing false references which caused the auto - stabiliser system to fly the aircraft into the ground . The failure occurred at less than 30 feet , giving pilot J.R. Green no time to revert to manual control . Airframe impacted inverted , killing pilot .
