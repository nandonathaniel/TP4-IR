IEC 60320 - 1 : General Requirements specifies two - pole and two - pole with earth couplers intended for the connection of a supply cord to electrical appliances . As from IEC 60320 - 1 : 2015 this part also applies to interconnection couplers which enable the connection and disconnection of an appliance to a cord leading to another appliance . This part of the standard no longer includes standard sheets which have been moved to a new part first published in 2014 : IEC 60320 - 3 .
