In Germany , anti-smoking groups , often associated with anti-liquor groups , first published advocacy against the consumption of tobacco in the journal Der Tabakgegner ( The Tobacco Opponent ) , by the Bohemian organization between 1912 and 1932 . The Deutsche Tabakgegner ( German Tobacco Opponents ) was published in Dresden from 1919 to 1935 , and was the second journal on this subject .
