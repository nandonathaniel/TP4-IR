In 2012 , the BSA reaffirmed its policies on the exclusion of homosexuals ; again a number of Eagle Scouts returned their badges in protest . In May 2013 , the National Council of the Boy Scouts of America voted to lift the ban on openly gay youth beginning on January 1 , 2014 , with the ban on openly gay adult leaders remaining in effect . On June 27 , 2015 , the ban on gay leaders was also lifted .
