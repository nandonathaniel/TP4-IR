Chlumsky appeared as Mary Calvin in a season 17 episode of Law & Order , first airing on January 12 , 2007 ; and as Lisa Klein in a season 20 episode , first airing March 15 , 2010 . In March 2007 , she appeared in the 30 Rock episode `` The Fighting Irish '' as Liz Lemler , a romantic rival of protagonist Liz Lemon , who receives flowers meant for Lemler . Chlumsky was in four episodes of the ABC dramedy Cupid in 2009 .
