Mary Alice Crosswire , known better by her nickname Muffy ( Melissa Altro ) , is an orangutan who usually wears a purple & white dress . She is the spoiled daughter of Ed Crosswire , a wealthy entrepreneur , and has an older brother named Chip . Because of her wealthy background , Muffy can be rather haughty and often tries to take credit for other people 's work . Nevertheless , she usually learns her lessons in hubris and enjoys spending time with her friends outside the family mansion .
