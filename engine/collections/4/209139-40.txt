Brydon Coverdale -- Appeared on several Australian quiz programs , including Million Dollar Minute where he won $307,000 in 2014 , becoming the first grand champion of Pass the Buck where he won $38,788 in 2002 . He also won a total of $32,000 on Who Wants to Be a Millionaire ? , appeared on The Weakest Link in 2001 , Deal or No Deal in 2005 and was a quarter - finalist on Letters and Numbers in 2011 . Nicknamed `` The Shark '' .
