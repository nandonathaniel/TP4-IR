Yuvraj was born in a Sikh family to Yograj Singh , a former India cricketer , and Shabnam Singh . Tennis and roller skating were Yuvraj 's favorite sports during his childhood and he was quite good at both . He had also won the National Under - 14 Roller Skating Championship . His father threw away the medal and told him to forget skating and concentrate on cricket . He would take Yuvraj to training every day .
