Hayley Hasselhoff has been modeling since 2007 when she was 14 . She is considered a plus - size model as she is a size 14 . She has been signed with Wilhelmina Models and as of 2014 , is signed with Ford Models . She has walked in shows such as the 2014 British Plus Size Fashion Week , and has modeled for Torrid . She has also been named the ambassador for Pulp Fashion Week in Paris .
