A poll in 2003 among Taiwanese residents aged between 13 and 22 found that , when given the options of either becoming a province of the People 's Republic of China or a state within the U.S. , 55 % of the respondents preferred statehood while only 36 % chose joining China . A group called Taiwan Civil Government , established in Taipei in 2008 , claims that the island of Taiwan and other minor islands are the territory of the United States .
