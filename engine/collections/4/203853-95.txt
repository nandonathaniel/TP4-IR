Crankcase is the leader of the Dreads , and he specializes in stealing information . Crankcase was killed by the Autobot Ironhide with the help of Sideswipe . Crowbar ( voiced by Jimmie Wood ) specializes in getting into secured areas and systems . He is killed by Ironhide . Hatchet is a master strategist for the Decepticons , who has an animal - like robot mode . He is killed by Bumblebee and Dino on the highway .
