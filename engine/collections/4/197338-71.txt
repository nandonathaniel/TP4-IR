Arnott continues to meet with Lindsay Denton as they investigate the possibility of Danny Waldron hiding another copy of his list of abusers . Denton realises the list is likely kept digitally , so she tricks Arnott into pursuing a false lead while she , after stealing Arnott 's pocketbook , goes to an internet cafe to access Waldron 's emails . She finds a photograph of the list in his drafts folder and transfers it to hers as insurance .
