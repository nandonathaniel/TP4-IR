The effects of Hurricane Katrina in New Orleans cause handyman and blues singer - songwriter Gabe Recolte ( Keb ' Mo ' ) to be homeless and broken hearted . The storm arrived before he could express his love for Hattie , a club owner . Forced to evacuate and relocate , Gabe writes a heartfelt letter to her . Years later , the Postables face unusual challenges in solving the mystery . Oliver also grapples with his unexpressed feelings for Shane ; while Norman and Rita 's relationship continues to get stronger .
