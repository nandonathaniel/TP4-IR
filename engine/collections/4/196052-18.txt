Fuerteventura ( pronounced ( fweɾteβenˈtuɾa ) ; literally meaning `` strong fortune '' , is one of the Canary Islands , in the Atlantic Ocean and is part of the North Africa region , politically part of Spain . At 1,660 square kilometres ( 641 square miles ) , it is the second largest of the Canary Islands , after Tenerife . It was declared a biosphere reserve by UNESCO in May 2009 . Its capital is Puerto del Rosario .
