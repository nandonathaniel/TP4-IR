At the time of California 's admission into the Union , travel between California and the rest of the continental United States had been a time consuming and dangerous feat . Nineteen years afterwards , in 1869 , shortly after the conclusion of the Civil War , a more direct connection was developed with the completion of the First Transcontinental Railroad at Promontory Summit , Utah . Once completed , hundreds of thousands of United States citizens were enabled to easily migrate westwards into the state .
