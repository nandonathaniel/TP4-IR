HMT Empire Windrush , originally MV Monte Rosa , was a passenger liner and cruise ship launched in Germany in 1930 . She was operated as a German cruise ship under the name Monte Rosa in the 1930s , and as a German navy troopship during World War II . At the end of the war , she was acquired by the United Kingdom Government as a prize of war and renamed the Empire Windrush . In British service , she continued to be used mainly as a troopship until March 1954 , when the vessel caught fire and sank in the Mediterranean Sea with the loss of four crew .
