Mourinho met his wife Matilde `` Tami '' Faria , born in Angola , when they were teenagers in Setúbal , Portugal , and the couple married in 1989 . Their first child , daughter Matilde , was born in 1996 and they had their first son , José Mário , Jr. , who plays football for Fulham 's youth team , four years later . Mourinho , whilst dedicated to football , describes his family as the centre of his life and has noted that the `` most important thing is my family and being a good father . '' He was selected as the New Statesman Man of the Year 2005 and was described as a man devoted to both his family and his work .
