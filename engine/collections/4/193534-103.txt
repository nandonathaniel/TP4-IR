On March 31 , 2015 , lead singer Steven Tyler stated that he was working on his first solo country album . On April 6 , it was announced that Tyler signed a record deal with Scott Borchetta 's Dot Records ( a division of the Big Machine Label Group ) . On May 13 , Tyler released the lead single , `` Love is Your Name '' , from his forthcoming solo debut album . He promoted the song on the Bobby Bones Show , iHeartMedia , CBS This Morning , Entertainment Tonight , and the American Idol season 14 finale .
