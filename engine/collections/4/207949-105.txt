NFL London Games : Three games will be played in London in 2018 . This will be the first season of a 10 - year agreement to play at least one game per season at the new Tottenham Stadium , where the Oakland Raiders will host the Seattle Seahawks on October 14 . Two more games will be played at Wembley Stadium , with the Jacksonville Jaguars hosting the Philadelphia Eagles and the Los Angeles Chargers hosting the Tennessee Titans . These two games will be played October 21 and 28 , with the order to be determined when the rest of the 2018 schedule is announced . This will be the first time the London games will be played on three consecutive weeks , a move the league has taken to try to streamline their London operations .
