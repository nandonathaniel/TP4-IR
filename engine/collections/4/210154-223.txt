000000001987 - 02 - 04 - 0000 February 4 , 1987 Northridge , California ! Northridge , California 0 35 - year - old associate professor of Computer Science Djamshid ( Amir ) Asgari was confronted in the Engineering Building of California State University , Northridge by 25 - year - old graduate student Fawwaz Abdin . Abdin was angry about a low grade Asgari had given him a year earlier , which caused him to be put on academic probation . After Asgari refused to change his grade , Abdin shot him twice , then himself . Asgari later died at the Northridge Hospital .
