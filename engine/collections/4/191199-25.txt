Freeway : A divided major roadway with full control of access and with no crossings at grade . This definition applies to toll as well as toll - free roads . Freeway A : This designates roadways with greater visual complexity and high traffic volumes . Usually this type of freeway will be found in metropolitan areas in or near the central core and will operate through much of the early evening hours of darkness at or near design capacity . Freeway B : This designates all other divided roadways with full control of access where lighting is needed .
