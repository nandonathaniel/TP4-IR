FE ( Falange Española de las JONS ) - created by a merger in 1934 of two fascist organisations , Primo de Rivera 's Falange ( Phalanx ) , founded in 1933 , and Ramiro Ledesma 's Juntas de Ofensiva Nacional - Sindicalista ( Assemblies of National - Syndicalist Offensive ) , founded in 1931 . It became a mass movement when it was joined by members of Acción Popular and by Acción Católica , led by Ramón Serrano Súñer . OJE ( Organización Juvenil Española ) - militant youth movement . Sección Femenina ( Feminine Section ) - women 's movement in labour of Social Aid .
