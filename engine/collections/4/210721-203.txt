Lee 's first audition was with `` Every Breath You Take '' and `` I Still Have n't Found What I 'm Looking For '' . At the bootcamp , he sang `` Sweet Child O'Mine '' and at the judges ' houses stage , sang `` Without You '' . He was eliminated from the live shows on 17 October , after singing `` Born to Run '' and receiving the fewest public votes for week 2 . He finished 14th overall . His album Soulfillapopkilla was re-released on 1 March 2011 .
