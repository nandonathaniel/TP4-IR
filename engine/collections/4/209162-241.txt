Five featurettes `` Trip to the Big Screen '' , a 12 - minute look at the film 's pre-production , discussing the changes made from the novel , how Frankel was chosen to direct and other issues . `` NYC and Fashion '' , a look at the real New York fashion scene and how it is portrayed in the film . `` Fashion Visionary Patricia Field '' , a profile of the film 's costume designer . `` Getting Valentino '' , covering how the designer was persuaded to appear as himself in the film . `` Boss from Hell '' , a short segment on difficult , nightmarish superiors like Priestly .
