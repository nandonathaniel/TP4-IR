These defences were augmented by the presence in the Suez Canal of HMS Swiftsure , HMS Clio , HMS Minerva the armed merchant cruiser HMS Himalaya and HMS Ocean near Qantara , Ballah , Sallufa , Gurka Post and Esh Shatt respectively , with the French protected cruiser D'Entrecasteaux just north of the Great Bitter Lake , the HMS Proserpine at Port Said , the Royal Indian Marine Ship Hardinge south of Lake Timsah and north of Tussum , with the French coastal defence ship Requin in Lake Timsah . The canal was closed each night during the threat .
