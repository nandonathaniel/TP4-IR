In German , Alfa - Echo ( ae ) may be used for `` ä '' , Oscar - Echo ( oe ) for `` ö '' , Sierra - Sierra ( ss ) for `` ß '' , and Uniform - Echo ( ue ) for `` ü '' . The same applies to `` ä '' and `` ö '' in Swedish , though Ärlig and Östen , from the Swedish spelling alphabet , are also used ; similarly , Åke may be used for `` å '' .
