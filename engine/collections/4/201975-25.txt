Georgia 5 illegal cannabis oil less than 5 % THC medical use only illegal Main article : Cannabis in Georgia ( U.S. state ) Any conviction of a marijuana possession , sale , or cultivation offense results in suspension of driver 's license . First - time offenders may be eligible for a conditional discharge under Section 16 - 13 - 2 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated ( O.C.G.A. ) , which operates as a dismissal if certain conditions are met , such as the payment of a fine and community service . A measure to allow medical cannabis oil passed the House in February 2015 . On April 16 , 2015 , the non-psychoactive form of Marijuana oil ( CBD Oil , also known as `` Charlotte 's Web '' ) was legalized for medical use in the state .
