During the Nazi occupation of France ( 1940 -- 44 ) , the franc was a satellite currency of the German Reichsmark . The exchange rate was 20 francs for 1 RM . The coins were changed , with the words Travail , famille , patrie ( Work , Family , Fatherland ) replacing the Republican triad Liberté , égalité , fraternité ( Liberty , Equality , Fraternity ) and the emblem of the Vichy regime added .
