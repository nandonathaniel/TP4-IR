On 30 October 2009 , Wise announced his resignation from Rare and his intention to work as a freelance composer / sound designer . In December 2010 Wise created a personal studio called the ' David Wise Sound Studio ' . In June 2013 it was announced that after eleven years without composing for a major console title , Wise would be returning to the series that made him famous with Donkey Kong Country : Tropical Freeze .
