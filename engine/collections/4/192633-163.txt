Many sailing events are held in the waters of this country , the largest of which is a week - long series of races called the Spring Regatta , the premier sailing event of the Caribbean , with several races hosted each day . Boats include everything from full - size mono - hull yachts to dinghies . Captains and their crews come from all around the world to attend these races . The Spring Regatta is part race , part party , part festival . There are races , games , and music during the day , and some partying at night . The Spring Regatta is normally held during the first week of April .
