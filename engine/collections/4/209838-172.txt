In the United States and Canada , Pitch Perfect 2 grossed $4.6 million from Thursday night showings and $27.8 million on its opening day , increasing the opening weekend projections from $40 million to $64 million . In its opening weekend , the film grossed $69.2 million , finishing first at the box office . The opening weekend gross was more than the entire North American total gross of the first film ( $65 million ) , is the third biggest PG - 13 comedy opening of all - time ( behind The Simpsons Movie 's $74 million in 2007 and Austin Powers in Goldmember 's $73 million in 2002 ) and the highest grossing opening ever for a musical .
