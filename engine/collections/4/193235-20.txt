`` You Can Do It '' is a song by American rapper Ice Cube . It was released as the second single from the Next Friday soundtrack . The song features Ice Cube 's Westside Connection bandmate Mack 10 , as well as female rapper Ms. Toi . `` You Can Do It '' later used as the lead single on Cube 's sixth studio album , War & Peace Vol. 2 ( The Peace Disc ) . The song also appears on his Greatest Hits and In the Movies compilations . It would also appear on the double platinum Save the Last Dance .
