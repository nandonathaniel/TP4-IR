Born in Tompkins County , New York . USMA , 1842 . Mexican -- American War . Wife from Athens , Georgia . Resigned as captain , U.S. Army , April 1 , 1861 . Major , CSA Corps of Engineers , March 16 , 1861 . Engineer , July 22 , 1861 -- April 11 , 1862 . 21st Louisiana Infantry : colonel , January 30 , 1862 . Planned defenses and commanded troops at New Orleans and Vicksburg . Captured at Vicksburg . A paroled prisoner of war for seven months until February 1864 . Chief engineer of Army of Northern Virginia , then Army of Tennessee . Prepared defenses of Mobile under Beauregard . Died July 29 , 1866 , Savannah , Georgia , aged 46 .
