Growing commercial , residential , and light industrial areas , are found all over the city at major road intersections and along major thoroughfares . However , the bulk of the San Jose del Monte 's built - up areas are mostly west of Quirino Highway at the primary level to gently sloping 8 % terrain . Most of the city 's schools , government institutions , commercial developments , industries , and other urban amenities are in this section . The largest contiguous built - up area is at Sapang Palay Resettlement Project area , followed by the conurbation in Tungkong Mangga and Muzon .
