Temples of modern India was a term coined by India 's first Prime Minister Jawahar Lal Nehru while starting the construction of the Bhakra Nangal Dam to describe scientific research institutes , steel plants , power plants , dams being launched in India after independence to jumpstart scientific and industrial progress. Dams are known as temples of modern India . Temples of Modern India , Financial Express , August 16 , 2003. < / ref > These projects were part of his vision of development of modern India with a mix of heavy industries and scientific research institutes .
