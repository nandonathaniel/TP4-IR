In the following season , in the last regular season meeting between the Avalanche and the Red Wings on March 26 , 1997 , a brawl known as the Brawl in Hockeytown broke out . The game ended with nine fights , 11 goals , 39 penalties , 148 penalty minutes , one hat - trick ( by Valeri Kamensky ) and a goaltender fight between Stanley Cup champion Patrick Roy and Mike Vernon . Claude Lemieux was one of the players singled out by the Red Wings players . The Red Wings ended up winning the game in overtime 6 -- 5 . The teams met again in the Western Conference Finals that season , with the Red Wings emerging victorious and going on to win the Stanley Cup .
