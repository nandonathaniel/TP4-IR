Acting Isaac H. Edgett April 15 , 1911 -- April 28 , 1911 . Republican Edgett , was the First Deputy Secretary of the Commonwealth under William M. Olin . As the First Deputy Secretary of the Commonwealth Edgett became the acting Secretary of the Commonwealth upon Olin 's death . Edgett served as the acting Secretary of the Commonwealth until Albert P. Langtry was elected by the Massachusetts legislature to serve out the remainder of Olin 's term .
