March 11 : Mikhail Gorbachev becomes leader of the Soviet Union . August 6 : Coinciding with the 40th anniversary of the atomic bombing of Hiroshima and Nagasaki , the Soviet Union begins what it has announced is a 5 - month unilateral moratorium on the testing of nuclear weapons . The Reagan administration dismisses the dramatic move as nothing more than propaganda and refuses to follow suit . Gorbachev declares several extensions , but the United States fails to reciprocate , and the moratorium comes to an end on February 5 , 1987 . November 21 : Reagan and Gorbachev meet for the first time at a summit in Geneva , Switzerland , where they agree to two ( later three ) more summits .
