A 3 - year ( or longer ) period of research . Extensions may be requested for maximum 5 years . The student must write his thesis presenting a new discovery or original contribution to science . If approved by her or his `` thesis director ( or directors ) '' , the study will be presented to a panel of 5 distinguished scholars . Any doctor attending the public presentations is allowed to challenge the candidate with questions on his research . If approved , he will receive the doctorate . Four marks can be granted : Unsatisfactory , Pass , Satisfactory , and Excellent . `` Cum laude '' ( with all honours , in Latin ) denomination can be added to the Excellent ones if all five members of the tribunal agree .
