On the opening play of the game , Arizona State 's Katie Hempen hit a three - pointer . The next time down the court , she hit another three - pointer and would go on to hit all six of her attempts , tying an NCAA record . The Aggies from New Mexico State fell behind early and never caught up . The Aggies were in their second consecutive NCAA tournament . Arizona State earned the two seed , the highest they have received in the history of the program . The Sun Devils won the game 74 -- 52 .
