Barbie 's direct - to - DVD animated films have sold more than 27 million units worldwide , as of 2006 . In addition , the brand has had two television specials , Barbie and the Rockers : Out of This World and Barbie and the Sensations : Rockin ' Back to Earth , as well as a hit song , `` Barbie Girl '' ( 1997 ) by Aqua . She is also a supporting character in the Pixar films Toy Story 2 and Toy Story 3 .
