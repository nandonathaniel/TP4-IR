Constance Contraire is a small , extremely intelligent , stubborn and often annoying girl . She has the ability to write clever and brutal poems , and her ultra-sensitive mind is also the most severely affected by the hidden messages transmitted by the Whisperer . Reynie , Sticky and Kate find Constance to be stubborn , petulant and often immature . At the end of the book , it is revealed that she is only two and a half years old , which explains her behavior . Also , at the end of the book , Mr. Benedict offers to adopt her .
