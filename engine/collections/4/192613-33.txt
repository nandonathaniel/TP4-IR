Following the Atlantic Records signing , the group released two EPs of new material . On October 29 , 2013 , they released their Overtime EP , consisting of six new songs , including `` Take Me Home '' , `` Hideaway '' , featuring British singer Ella Eyre and `` Here and Now '' , featuring vocals from Estonian singer Kerli . On March 24 , 2014 , the group released the Lightning EP , consisting of a new single `` Lightning '' and remixes of previous songs off the Overtime EP . `` Lightning '' features John Rzeznik of the Goo Goo Dolls on vocals and was written by Cash Cash and Rzeznik . A remix pack for `` Lightning '' was released on July 21 , 2014 exclusively on beatport containing remixes by Dash Berlin , EDX , Sex Panther , and Audiobot .
