The series follows the adventures of three siblings called the Baudelaire orphans . Snicket explains that very few positive things happen to the children , but much misfortune befalls them . Violet Baudelaire , the eldest , is 14 when the series begins and is an inventor . Klaus Baudelaire , the middle child , is twelve when the books begin ; he loves books and is an extraordinary speed reader who can remember everything he has ever read after reading it only once . Sunny Baudelaire is a baby in the beginning of the series , and enjoys biting things ; she develops a love for cooking later in the series .
