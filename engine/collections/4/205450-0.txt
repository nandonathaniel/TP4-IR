Area code 951 is a California telephone area code that was split from area code 909 on July 17 , 2004 . It covers western Riverside County , including , Beaumont , Corona , Canyon Lake , Riverside , Temescal Canyon , Woodcrest , Arlington , Mira Loma , Moreno Valley , Perris , Sun City , Lake Elsinore , Wildomar , Murrieta , Temecula , San Jacinto , Hemet , Lakeview , Nuevo , Norco , Banning , Eastvale , Quail Valley , and Idyllwild .
