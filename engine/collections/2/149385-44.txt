Del Toro also wanted the animals to speak so that Smaug 's speech would not be incongruous , though he explained portraying the talking animals would be more about showing that other characters can understand them . Smaug would not have a `` snub Simian ( mouth ) in order to achieve a dubious lip - synch '' . Del Toro stated that Smaug would be the first character design begun and the last to be approved .
