Uma speaks with Ben , bitter that she and the others were not chosen to go to Auradon . Ben takes this into account and invites her to Auradon , but Uma instead plots to make her own way there . Mal 's group return , handing over the fake wand in exchange for Ben , but Uma realizes it is a forgery . The group flee back to Auradon , but Mal and Ben 's relationship is still on the rocks . The villains ' children come to terms that they can not run from their pasts and agree to be honest with themselves and each other . Carlos confesses to Jane but struggles a little bit , while Jay appoints Lonnie as the captain of his team .
