Bridges originally auditioned for the role of Mitchie Torres in the first Camp Rock film , but it ultimately went to Demi Lovato . She also appeared in the 2009 films Legally Blondes and Forget Me Not . She secured the role of Dana Turner in the sequel , Camp Rock 2 : The Final Jam , which aired on Disney Channel in 2010 . In March 2012 , Bridges was cast as Donna LaDonna in The CW 's Sex and the City prequel , titled The Carrie Diaries , which aired from 2013 to 2014 over two seasons .
