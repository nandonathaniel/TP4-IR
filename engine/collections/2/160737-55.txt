Development for a crossover between the three series , along with Supergirl , began in May 2016 after it was revealed that Supergirl would move to The CW from CBS for its second season . By the following September , the scripts for each episode had been written , with filming occurring shortly after , in October 2016 . The premise and episode titles were also revealed that month . `` Invasion ! '' sees all of the main cast members for each series appear in at least their own series , with Melissa Benoist appearing as Supergirl across the entire crossover . Multiple actors and characters from previous Arrow seasons also return to reprise their roles , as Arrow 's episode doubled as its 100th .
