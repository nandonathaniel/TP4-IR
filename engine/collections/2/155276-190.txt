Many large firms expanded dramatically . The workforce at Ansaldo grew from 6,000 to 110,000 as it manufactures 10,900 artillery pieces , 3,800 warplanes , 95 warships and 10 million artillery shells . At Fiat the workforce grew from 4,000 to 40,000 . Inflation doubled the cost of living . Industrial wages kept pace but not wages for farm workers . Discontent was high in rural areas since so many men were taken for service , industrial jobs were unavailable , wages grew slowly and inflation was just as bad .
