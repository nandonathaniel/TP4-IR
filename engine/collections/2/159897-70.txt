The members of Rage Against the Machine are well known for their leftist and revolutionary political views , and almost all of the band 's songs focus on these views . Key to the band 's identity , Rage Against the Machine has voiced viewpoints highly critical of the domestic and foreign policies of current and previous U.S. governments . Throughout its existence , Rage Against the Machine and its individual members participated in political protests and other activism to advocate these beliefs . The band sees its music as a vehicle for social activism ; De la Rocha explained , `` I 'm interested in spreading those ideas through art , because music has the power to cross borders , to break military sieges and to establish real dialogue .
