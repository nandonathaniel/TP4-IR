Greenland has a population of 56,370 ( January 2013 estimate ) , of whom 88 % are Greenlandic Inuit ( including mixed persons ) . The remaining 12 % are of European descent , mainly Greenland Danes . Several thousand Greenlandic Inuit reside in the Danish Penisula . The majority of the population is Lutheran . Nearly all Greenlanders live along the fjords in the south - west of the main island , which has a relatively mild climate . More than 17,000 people reside in Nuuk , the capital city .
