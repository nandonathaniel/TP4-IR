Cleveland is located in southeast Tennessee in the center of Bradley County situated among a series of low hills and ridges roughly 15 miles ( 24 km ) west of the Blue Ridge Mountains and 15 miles ( 24 km ) east of the Chickamauga Lake impoundment of the Tennessee River . The Hiwassee River , which flows down out of the mountains and forms the northern boundary of Bradley County , empties into the Tennessee a few miles northwest of Cleveland . According to the United States Census Bureau , the city had a total land area of 26.9 square miles ( 69.7 km ) in 2010 .
