The following is a list of episodes from the series Voltron : Legendary Defender . The first season was released on June 10 , 2016 . The first episode in Netflix is triple - length , creating a cumulative runtime for the season equal to the industry standard of 13 episodes , while other streaming services such as iTunes and Amazon have three separated episodes with the normal length of 23 minutes . The second season was released on January 20 , 2017 , the third season was released on August 4 , 2017 and the fourth season was released on October 13 , 2017 . The fifth season will be released on March 2 , 2018 .
