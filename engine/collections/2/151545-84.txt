While the economic stimulus bill did allow additional funds for coupons , there was also a risk that available retail stock of the converter boxes themselves could prove inadequate . The Consumer Electronics Association had estimated three to six million boxes remained in - stock at the beginning of February 2009 ; Nielsen Media Research reported five million households as `` completely unready '' for digital transition in this same time period . The average U.S. household uses 3 television screens . However , the converter box coupon program only allows 2 coupons per household .
