Jughead Jones is one of the main characters created by Bob Montana and John L. Goldwater in Archie Comics who first appeared in the first Archie story , from Pep Comics # 22 ( December 1941 ) . He is the son of Forsythe Jones II ; in one of the early Archie newspaper comic strips , he himself is identified as Forsythe Pendleton Jones III ( and in one strip , likely due to continuity error , as Forsythe Van Jones ) . He has a white sheepdog named Hot Dog and a younger sister , Forsythia `` Jellybean '' Jones .
