Diplomatic passport ( Turkish : Diplomatik pasaport ) , after 2010 also referred to as Black Passport ( Turkish : Siyah pasaport ) . It is issued to ; Members of the National Assembly , and the non-PM ministers Head judges of the judicial organs , and their deputies Diplomats Negotiators that are to deal with international issues Spouse of the diplomatic passport holders . However , this passport can only be used when accompanying the primary passport holder . Unwed and unemployed daughters , and sons younger than 18 years of age of the diplomatic passport holders who live with parents . However , this passport can only be used when accompanying the primary passport holder .
