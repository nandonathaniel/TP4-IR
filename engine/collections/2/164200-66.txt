1762 -- 1764 Flag during the brief British occupation of Manila , as used in occupied Manila and Cavite . The flag of the British East India Company before 1810 : A flag with red and white stripes with the Kingdom of Great Britain 's Union Flag as a canton . The Union flag bears red cross on a white field , commonly called St George 's Cross , superimposed on a white saltire on a blue field , known as St Andrew 's Cross . Also known as the `` King 's Colours '' .
