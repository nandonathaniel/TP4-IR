Stefanie Maria `` Steffi '' Graf ( German pronunciation : ( ˈʃtɛfiː ˈgʁa : f ) ; born 14 June 1969 ) is a German former professional tennis player . She was ranked world No. 1 and won 22 Grand Slam singles titles . Her 22 singles titles put her second on the list of major wins in the female competition since the introduction of the Open Era in 1968 and is third all - time behind Margaret Court ( 24 ) and Serena Williams ( 23 ) . In 1988 , she became the first and only tennis player ( male or female ) to achieve the Golden Slam by winning all four Grand Slam singles titles and the Olympic gold medal in the same calendar year . Furthermore , she is the only tennis player to have won each Grand Slam tournament at least four times .
