In Minnesota , it is illegal for a child under the age of 14 to possess an airsoft gun unless under the supervision of a parent or adult . It is also illegal for any child under 16 to purchase an airsoft gun without parental advisory involvement . In Saint Paul and Minneapolis , airsoft guns can not be carried in public unless they either have an orange tip or the airsoft guns are clear or brightly colored . It is legal to possess airsoft guns in these cities as long as they are transported in a closed and fastened gun case ( in accordance with Minnesota firearm transportation laws ) and unloaded . The vast majority of municipalities ( Excluding Duluth and Rochester ) in Minnesota ban the firing of an airsoft gun within the city limits .
