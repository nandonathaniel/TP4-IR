The mushroom has a good shelf life . An effective cultivation method was introduced to Japan around 1993 . The mushroom has become popular there in a variety of dishes , and is now also cultivated and sold commercially in Australia . Imported product is also commercially available in Australia and South Africa . It is also cultivated in United Kingdom , Taiwan , China , South Korea , Italy , and the United States . It has little flavor or aroma when raw . When cooked , it develops typical mushroom umami flavors with a texture similar to that of abalone .
