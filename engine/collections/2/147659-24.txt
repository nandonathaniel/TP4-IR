Federal Stafford Loans are made to students and PLUS Loans are made to parents through the William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan ( Direct Loan ) Program : Eligible students and parents borrow directly from the federal government at participating schools . Direct Loans include Direct Stafford Loans , Direct PLUS Loans , and Direct Consolidation Loans . The Federal Family Education Loan ( FFEL ) Program : Until July 1 , 2010 , a large percentage of federal loans were made through private lenders with federally guaranteed funds . FFEL loans included FFEL Stafford Loans , FFEL PLUS Loans , and FFEL Consolidation Loans . The FFEL Program ended on July 1 , 2010 under provisions of the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010 . All new Stafford , PLUS , and consolidation loans are now administered through the Direct Loan Program .
