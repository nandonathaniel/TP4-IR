Sochi , Russia , was selected as the host city of the 2014 Winter Olympics over Salzburg , Austria , and Pyeongchang , South Korea . This was the first time that Russia hosted a Winter Olympics . Over 2800 athletes from 88 countries participated in 98 events . The Olympic Village and Olympic Stadium were located on the Black Sea coast . All of the mountain venues were 50 kilometres ( 31 mi ) away in the alpine region known as Krasnaya Polyana .
