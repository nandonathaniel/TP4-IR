How People View The Media : Two - thirds ( 67 % ) said agreed with the statement : `` In dealing with political and social issues , news organizations tend to favor one side . '' That was up 14 points from 53 percent who gave that answer in 1985 . Those who believed the media `` deal fairly with all sides '' fell from 34 percent to 27 percent . `` In one of the most telling complaints , a majority ( 54 % ) of Americans believe the news media gets in the way of society solving its problems , '' Pew reported . Republicans `` are more likely to say news organizations favor one side than are Democrats or independents ( 77 percent vs. 58 percent and 69 percent , respectively ) . '' The percentage who felt `` news organizations get the facts straight '' fell from 55 percent to 37 percent .
