Dohan river , tributary of Sahibi river , originates near Neem Ka Thana in Alwar district ) . Sota River , tributary of Sahibi river , merges with Sahibi river at Behror in Alwar district . Krishnavati river , former tributary of Sahibi river , originates near Dariba copper mines in Rajsamand district of Rajasthan , flows through Patan in Dausa district and Mothooka in Alwar district , then disappears in Mahendragarh district in Haryana much before reaching Sahibi river .
