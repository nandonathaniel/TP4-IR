The deadline for Eve to make a decision about college is approaching , causing Mike and Vanessa to worry when she keeps putting off the topic . Eve later confesses to Mike that she secretly applied to the Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs and was accepted . Now , the two have to figure out how to break the news to Vanessa that Eve will be entering the military . Meanwhile , Ed has to put down his dog Bogey . Kyle and Mandy decide to have Bogey stuffed so he can be around Ed forever , much to Ed 's shock .
