The starring troupe over all five series consisted of Mathew Baynton , Simon Farnaby , Martha Howe - Douglas , Jim Howick , Laurence Rickard and Ben Willbond , some combination of whom appear , with very rare exceptions , in every live - action sketch ; their voices can also be heard in the animated sketches . Supporting cast was headed by Sarah Hadland , Lawry Lewin and Dominique Moore .
