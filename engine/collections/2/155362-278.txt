The UK is closely associated with motorsport . Many teams and drivers in Formula One ( F1 ) are based in the UK , and the country has won more drivers ' and constructors ' titles than any other . The UK hosted the first F1 Grand Prix in 1950 at Silverstone , the current location of the British Grand Prix held each year in July . The UK hosts legs of the Grand Prix motorcycle racing , World Rally Championship and FIA World Endurance Championship . The premier national auto racing event is the British Touring Car Championship . Motorcycle road racing has a long tradition with races such as the Isle of Man TT and the North West 200 .
