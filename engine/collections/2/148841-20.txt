Width from grip to grip may vary from 30.5 to 37 inches ( 770 to 940 mm ) . May rise above triple clamp up to 24 in ( 610 mm ) or more , called ape hangers when very high , or may drop a few inches below , called clubman bars . Pullback , the distance grips are behind their mounting location , may vary from 4.25 to 17 in ( 108 to 432 mm ) . Diameters vary ; commonly ⁄ , 1 , and 1 ⁄ in ( 22 , 25 , and 32 mm ) , though oversized bars of 1 ⁄ , 1 ⁄ , and 1 ⁄ in ( 32 , 38 , and 44 mm ) may reduce to 1 in ( 25 mm ) at the grips so standard controls may be mounted .
