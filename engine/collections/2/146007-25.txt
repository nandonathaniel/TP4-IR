Actress McKenzie Westmore , known for her role as Sheridan Crane on the former NBC / DirecTV soap opera Passions and a member of the Westmore family of makeup artists , serves as the show 's host . For each assignment , the contestants ' work is individually evaluated by a panel of professional special effects makeup artists who serve as judges . These judges have included television and film makeup artist Glenn Hetrick , Academy Award and Emmy Award - winning makeup artist Ve Neill , creature designer and director Patrick Tatopoulos ( seasons 1 - 3 ) , and creature and concept designer Neville Page ( seasons 3 - present ) . Industry figures and artists including Brian Grazer , Kevin Smith , Paul W.S. Anderson , Greg Nicotero , Rick Baker , Doug Jones , Len Wiseman , Gale Anne Hurd and Marcus Nispel have served as guest judges .
