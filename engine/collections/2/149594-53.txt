One Canada Square 235 771 50 1991 The second - tallest completed building in the United Kingdom , the tallest being The Shard . Designed by Cesar Pelli , it was the tallest building in the United Kingdom upon completion in 1991 . Multi-tenanted ; occupiers include The Bank of New York Mellon , the CFA Institute , Clearstream , EEX ( European Energy Exchange ) , Euler Hermes , the International Sugar Organization , Mahindra Satyam , MetLife , Moody 's Analytics and Trinity Mirror .
