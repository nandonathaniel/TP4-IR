Reddy authored a book , Without Fear or Favour : Reminiscences and Reflections of a President , published in 1989 . The character Mahendranath , Chief Minister of the fictional state of Afrozabad in former Prime Minister PV Narasimha Rao 's novel , The Insider , is based on Reddy , portraying his career in Andhra Pradesh and his political rivalry with Kasu Brahmananda Reddy .
