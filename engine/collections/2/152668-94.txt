After discovering Team Echo was killed because they burned a heroin farm , Team Bravo pays a visit to the farm and find more than 10 million dollars hidden underground . According to the farmer , some men take money out of it regularly . They capture a 15 years - old boy , who claims he just heard about the money , and wanted some for his family . They follow him , and discover he 's really a girl , Dorri , that works as messenger for the Taliban to be able to care of her younger brother . She helps them set up a trap , but their objective is killed as a traitor . Mandy identifies the men as Al - Quaeda mercenaries , and wonder who 's the farm owner . Dorri offers to keep helping them in exchange for tickets to the USA . Meanwhile , Jason meets Amy , a private security specialist working on the base .
