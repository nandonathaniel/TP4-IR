After the conclusion of the fourth season and prior to the start of production on the fifth and final season , certain series regulars departed the series . Ruth Wilson requested to leave ahead of season four , and her character was killed off at the end of the season . In September 2018 , it was reported that Joshua Jackson would not return as a series regular for the final season , but could return as a guest star . In October 2018 , it was also confirmed that Catalina Sandino Moreno would not be a series regular for the final season .
