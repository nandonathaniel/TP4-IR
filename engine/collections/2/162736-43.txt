Assumption Cathedral and Monastery of the town - island of Sviyazhsk Tatarstan 55 ° 46 ′ 13 '' N 48 ° 39 ′ 10 '' E ﻿ / ﻿ 55.77028 ° N 48.65278 ° E ﻿ / 55.77028 ; 48.65278 ﻿ ( Assumption Cathedral of Sviyazhsk ) 2017 ii , iv 1525 16th century on The Assumption Cathedral is located in the town - island of Sviyazhsk and is part of the monastery of the same name . Situated at the confluence of the Volga , the Sviyaga and the Shchuka rivers , at the crossroads of the Silk and Volga routes , Sviyazhsk was founded by Ivan the Terrible in 1551 . It was from this outpost that he initiated the conquest of the Kazan Khanate . The cathedral 's frescoes are among the rarest examples of Eastern Orthodox mural paintings .
