Covenant Medical Center , located at 1447 North Harrison , is a 623 bed hospital with approximately 20 inpatient and outpatient facilities , including an emergency department that provides 85,000 visits per year . It 's the location for `` CMU Medical Education Partners '' , which offers various residency training programs . It also offers walk - in clinics and facilities scattered throughout the county for out - patient surgery , breast cancer diagnosis , physical therapy , sports medicine and eye care .
