The series was met with a positive response from critics . On the review aggregation website Rotten Tomatoes , the series holds a 88 % approval rating with an average rating of 7.73 out of 10 based on 34 reviews . The website 's critical consensus reads , `` Hayley Atwell shines in Howards End , a beautiful , if borderline superficial , adaptation of a much - revered book . '' Metacritic , which uses a weighted average , assigned the season a score of 89 out of 100 based on 10 critics , indicating `` universal acclaim '' .
