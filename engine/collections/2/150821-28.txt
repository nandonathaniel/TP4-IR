Scott Michael Foster as `` Cappie , '' later revealed to be named Captain John Paul Jones - The President of Kappa Tau Gamma and Casey 's ex-boyfriend from freshman year . Cappie eventually becomes Rusty 's `` big brother '' and introduces him to the rowdy Kappa Tau lifestyle . He loves college and Kappa Tau so much that he never wants to leave , and his immaturity continues to keep him and Casey apart .
