May was the first foreign leader to visit Trump after his inauguration , and she invited him to make a return visit . More than 1.8 million U.K citizens signed a petition to rescind the invitation , and Parliament debated a nonbinding resolution to that effect in February 2017 . The visit was tentatively planned for late February 2018 , and would include a ceremonial opening of the new American embassy in Nine Elms .
