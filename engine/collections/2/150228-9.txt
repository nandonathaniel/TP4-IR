Bill Monroe wrote the song in 1946 , recording it for Columbia Records on September 16 . It was released in early 1947 . At the time , the Bluegrass Boys included vocalist and guitarist Lester Flatt and banjoist Earl Scruggs , who later formed their own bluegrass band , the Foggy Mountain Boys . Both Flatt and Scruggs performed on the recording , although Bill Monroe supplied the vocals on this song .
