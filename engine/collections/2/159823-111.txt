Curry is an avid golfer ; he played golf in high school , and frequently plays golf with teammate Andre Iguodala . A 5 - handicap golfer , Curry participates in celebrity golf tournaments , and has played golf alongside Barack Obama . In August 2017 , Curry competed in the Ellie Mae Classic on an unrestricted sponsor exemption . Though he missed the first cut , he scored 4 - over-74 for both days he participated , surpassing most expectations for an amateur competing in the pro event .
