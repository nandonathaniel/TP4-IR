NWOHRs residing in South Korea are not required to apply for an entry permit to enter Taiwan prior to their travel . However , they must hold a ROC passport , a valid Alien Registration Card of South Korea , and apply for a Temporary Entry Permit upon arrival . The Alien Registration Card of South Korea of the NWOHR must denote the bearer 's immigration status in South Korea as either F - 2 ( Long - Term Residence ) or F - 5 ( Permanent Residence ) .
