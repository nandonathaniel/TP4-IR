Color blindness Synonyms Colour blindness , color deficiency , impaired color vision Example of an Ishihara color test plate . With properly configured computer displays , people with normal vision should see the number `` 74 '' . Many people who are color blind see it as `` 21 '' , and those with total color blindness may not see any numbers .
