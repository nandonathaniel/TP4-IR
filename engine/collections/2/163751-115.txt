In addition to players and builders , five broadcasters for the Montreal Canadiens were also awarded the Foster Hewitt Memorial Award from the Hockey Hall of Fame . The first two recipients of the award were Danny Gallivan and Rene Lecavalier in 1984 . The other three award recipients include Doug Smith ( 1985 ) , Dick Irvin ( 1988 ) , and Gilles Tremblay ( 2002 ) .
