Naruto is an anime series based on Masashi Kishimoto 's manga series of the same name . The series centers on the adventures of Naruto Uzumaki , a young ninja of the Hidden Leaf Village , searching for recognitions and wishing to become Hokage , the ninja that is acknowledged by the rest of the village to be the leader and the strongest of all . The series was directed by Hayato Date , and produced by Studio Pierrot and TV Tokyo . The episodes are based on the first twenty - seven volumes in Part I of the manga , while some episodes are just filler .
