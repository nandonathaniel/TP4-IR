`` Under the Sun '' , the second and final single from the album , was released on 3 September 2012 . The accompanying music video was released on 26 July 2012 , and it sees Cheryl travel back to the ' 50s to perform the track while strutting down the street . Further scenes see the singer draped on the bonnet of a classic car and performing a choreographed dance sequence with a handful of businessmen . After the release of the video the song peaked at number twenty - six , before the singles official release date . The song peaked at number 20 on the week ending 30 August 2012 , becoming her sixth solo top - twenty single . It has since peaked at number 13 .
