In the anime adaptation of the Japanese light novel series , Problem Children Are Coming from Another World , Are n't They ? ( 2013 ) , a major story revolves around the `` false legend '' of Pied Piper of Hamelin . The adaptation speaks in great length about the original source and the various versions of the story that sprang up throughout the years . It is stated that Weser , the representation of Natural Disaster , was the true Piper of Hamelin ( meaning the children were killed by drowning or landslides ) .
