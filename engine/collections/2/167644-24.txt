Meanwhile , Armando is being interrogated by Inspector Kolp ( Severn Darden ) , who suspects his `` circus ape '' is the child of the two talking apes from the future . Kolp 's assistant puts Armando under a machine , `` The Authenticator '' , that psychologically forces people to be truthful . After admitting he had heard the name Cornelius before , Armando realizes he can not fight the machine and jumps through a window to his death after a brief struggle with a guard . When Caesar learns of his foster father 's death , he loses faith in human kindness and begins secretly teaching the apes combat while having them gather weapons .
