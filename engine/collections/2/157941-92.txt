The Diesel / hydraulic powered `` work tractors '' are primarily used each morning for beam inspection and maintenance - of - way which includes trimming vegetation beyond the reach of guests , cleaning and repairing the electrical commutator rails as well as periodically scheduled servicing and painting of the concrete beam guideway . They are powerful enough to return a fully loaded train fitted with tow - bars to a station platform , then to the Monorail Shop , independent of 600V track power .
