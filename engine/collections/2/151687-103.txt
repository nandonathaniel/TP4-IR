Even his son Jahangir and grandson Shahjahan maintained many of Akbar 's concessions , such as the ban on cow slaughter , having only vegetarian dishes on certain days of the week , and drinking only Ganges water . Even as he was in the Punjab , 200 miles away from the Ganges , the water was sealed in large jars and transported to him . He referred to the Ganges water as the `` water of immortality . ''
