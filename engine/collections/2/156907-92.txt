Sinatra had met Barbato in Long Branch , New Jersey in the late 1930s , where he spent most of the summer working as a lifeguard . He agreed to marry her after an incident at `` The Rustic Cabin '' which led to his arrest . Sinatra had numerous extra-marital affairs , and gossip magazines published details of affairs with women including Marilyn Maxwell , Lana Turner , and Joi Lansing .
