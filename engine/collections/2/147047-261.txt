A Goddess sealed within Denzel Liones sword . Despite Denzel 's sacrifice to allow her to take possession of his body , she showed no care or empathy for the humans . When confronted by Derrierie for their brutal murder of demon hostages , she showed cowardice by claiming to be just following orders and tried to flee . This response enrages the Commandment and Nerobasta tries to beg for her life , only for the demon to kill both of them .
