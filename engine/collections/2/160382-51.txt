According to the Vietnamese government 's national survey and assessment of war casualties , there were 849,018 PAVN / VC military personnel deaths or missing during the Vietnam War ( including combat death and non-combat death ) from the period between 1955 and 1975 . It is unclear whether or not this figure includes the 300 - 330,000 PAVN / VC missing in action . One third of the deaths of the VC / NVA were non-combat deaths . Across all three wars . including the First Indochina War and the Third Indochina War there was a total of 1,146,250 combatant deaths : 191,605 in the First Indochina War , 849,018 in the Second Indochina War ( Vietnam War ) , and 105,627 in the Third Indochina War .
