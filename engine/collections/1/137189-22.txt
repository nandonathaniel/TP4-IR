Two years have passed since Dawson 's tragic death . Amanda and Frank have repaired their damaged marriage , though it was no longer romantic or casual as it was years prior . Dawson 's cousins , Abee and Crazy Ted have been arrested and put in jail for murdering Dawson . Amanda 's son is happy and healthy with his new heart and questions Amanda on the anniversary of his accident if it was possible to trace the donor and thank his family . Amanda , who knows that her son has received Dawson 's heart , after having investigated on hearing about Dawson 's death , tells him that it is made anonymous for a reason . She hugs him close tells him she loves him , which is meant for both Dawson whose heart is beating inside him as well as her son .
