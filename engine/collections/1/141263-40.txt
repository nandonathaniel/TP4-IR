Production of sequels was interrupted by Townsend 's declining health . At the time Adrian Mole -- The Weapons of Mass Destruction was published in 2004 , Townsend stated it would be the last Adrian Mole volume . However , in an interview on Leicester hospital station Radio Fox on 5 June 2008 , Townsend said that she was in fact writing a new Mole book entitled The Prostrate Years , which was released in 2009 . In October 2009 the Leicester Mercury featured an interview with Townsend where she discussed the new Mole book and her ( prospective ) plans for future works .
