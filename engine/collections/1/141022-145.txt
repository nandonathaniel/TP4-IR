The port city of Janggala or Hujung Galuh was one of the two Javanese capital city that was formed when Airlangga abdicated his throne in favour of his two sons in 1045 . The Kingdom of Janggala comprised the northeastern part of the Kingdom of Kahuripan . The other Kingdom was Kediri . Derived its name from the words `` suro '' ( shark ) and `` boyo '' ( crocodile ) , two creatures which are in a local myth .
