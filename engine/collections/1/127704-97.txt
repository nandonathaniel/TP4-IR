On November 28 , 2001 , Enron 's two worst - possible outcomes came true : Dynegy Inc. unilaterally disengaged from the proposed acquisition of the company , and Enron 's credit rating was reduced to junk status . Watson later said `` At the end , you could n't give it ( Enron ) to me . '' The company had very little cash with which to operate , let alone satisfy enormous debts . Its stock price fell to $0.61 at the end of the day 's trading . One editorial observer wrote that `` Enron is now shorthand for the perfect financial storm . ''
