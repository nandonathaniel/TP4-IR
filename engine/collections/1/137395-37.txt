Sailors of the day were notoriously against innovation , and at first the men would not eat the sauerkraut . Cook used a `` method I never once knew to fail with seamen . '' He ordered it served to himself and the officers , and left an option for crew who wanted some . Within a week of seeing their superiors set a value on it the demand was so great a ration had to be instituted . In other cases , however , Cook was required to resort to traditional naval discipline . `` Punished Henry Stephens , Seaman , and Thomas Dunster , Marine , with twelve lashes each for refusing to take their allowance of fresh beef . ''
