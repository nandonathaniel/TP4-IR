As the show progressed , numerous recurring guest stars appeared in the show . Michelle Trachtenberg signed on to portray Georgina Sparks . The role had previously been offered to Mischa Barton who passed . Francie Swift and Sam Robards took the parental roles of Anne and Howard Archibald , respectively . Caroline Lagerfelt portrayed Celia `` CeCe '' Rhodes , Serena and Eric 's grandmother and Lily 's mother . Sebastian Stan made several appearances as Carter Baizen throughout the show 's first three seasons .
