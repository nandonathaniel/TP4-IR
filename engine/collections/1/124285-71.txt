Ninth grade Students take the Math and Science RCT in June . This is done in case the students fail the Math and Living Environment Regents or Earth Science Regents which can be taken in 9th or 10th grade . Some students , however , can take Regents in 8th grade . Tenth grade Students take the Math and the Living Environment Regents or the Earth Science Regents in June . Students also take the Global History and Geography exam . Students can take the Global RCT if they failed the Global Regents . Eleventh grade Students take the Reading RCT in January . In June , students take the English and U.S. History Regents . Students can take the U.S. History RCT if they failed the U.S. History Regents . Twelfth grade Students take the Writing RCT in January if they have failed the English Regents .
