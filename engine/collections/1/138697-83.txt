Fired Up ! , a teen comedy about cheerleading camp , was released by Screen Gems in 2009 . In the supernatural horror - comedy Jennifer 's Body ( 2009 ) , Megan Fox plays a demonically possessed high school cheerleader . Also that year , Universal Pictures signed music video and film director Bille Woodruff ( Barbershop , Honey ) to direct the fifth film in the Bring It On series titled Bring It On : Fight to the Finish . The film stars Christina Milian ( who previously played cheerleaders in Love Do n't Cost a Thing and Man of the House ) and Rachelle Brook Smith , and was released directly to DVD and Blu - ray on September 1 , 2009 .
