The Bay Area is home to a complex network of watersheds , marshes , rivers , creeks , reservoirs , and bays that predominantly drain into the San Francisco Bay and Pacific Ocean . The largest bodies of water in the Bay Area are the San Francisco , San Pablo , and Suisun estuaries . Major rivers of the North Bay include the Napa River , the Petaluma River , the Gualala River , and the Russian River ; the former two drain into San Pablo Bay , the latter two into the Pacific Ocean . In the South Bay , the Guadalupe River drains into San Francisco Bay near Alviso . There are also several lakes present in the Bay Area , including man - made lakes like Lake Berryessa and natural albeit heavily modified lakes like Lake Merritt .
