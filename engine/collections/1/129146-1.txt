The expression `` servant of God '' appears eight times in the Bible , the first four in the Old Testament , the last four in the New . The Hebrew Bible refers to `` Moses the servant of Elohim '' ( עֶֽבֶד הָאֱלֹהִ֛ים ' eḇeḏ - hā'ĕlōhîm ; 1 Chronicles 6 : 49 , 2 Chronicles 24 : 9 , Nehemiah 10 : 29 , and Daniel 9 : 11 ) . Judges 2 : 8 refers to Joshua as ' eḇeḏ Yahweh ( עֶ֣בֶד יְהוָ֑ה ) .
