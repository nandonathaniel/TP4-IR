During 2011 , Humphries travelled to New Zealand to perform the role of the Goblin King in the first instalment of Peter Jackson 's three - part adaptation of J.R.R. Tolkien 's The Hobbit . The first film was released in December 2012 , with the other two parts due in December 2013 and December 2014 .
