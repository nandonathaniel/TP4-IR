Gears of War 2 is a third - person shooter video game published by Microsoft Studios . It is the sequel to Gears of War , and was announced by lead designer Cliff Bleszinski during the February 20 , 2008 Game Developers Conference . It was released worldwide November 7 , 2008 . The game uses a heavily upgraded version of the Unreal Engine 3 . Gears of War 2 takes place after the first game , where the Locust are now attempting to sink Jacinto Plateau , and the COG forces have decided to launch a counter-offensive to stop them before they can complete their attempt . Ultimately the COG sinks Jacinto themselves to flood the home of the Locust ( the Hollow ) and drown them .
