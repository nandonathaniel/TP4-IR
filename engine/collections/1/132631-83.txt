June 23 -- 27 - Tropical Storm Debby made landfall near Steinhatchee , Florida with winds of around 40 mph . Record - breaking inland / river flooding had occurred throughout North Florida and the system 's outer bands also helped spawn an unusually widespread tornado outbreak for South Florida . One tornado killed a woman in Lake Placid . Widespread rainfall amounts of 1 to 2 feet fell in the Florida Panhandle , isolated amounts of up to around 29 inches was recorded in Wakulla County . A total of 7 deaths were confirmed in Florida with damage amounting up to $45.3 million ( 2017 USD ) .
