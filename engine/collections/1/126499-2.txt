Indian painting has a very long tradition and history in Indian art . The earliest Indian paintings were the rock paintings of pre-historic times , the petroglyphs as found in places like Bhimbetka rock shelters , some of the Stone Age rock paintings found among the Bhimbetka rock shelters are approximately 30,000 years old . The painting history can be broadly categorized into three categories : mural painting , miniature painting and painting on other removable media ( fabrics , papers , wood , scrolls ... ) .
