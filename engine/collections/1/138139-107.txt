Jack Hamilton Marone Biological son of Brooke and Nick Marone , but he was carried by Taylor Hamilton . Wanting Nick back after he got engaged to Taylor , Brooke attempted to give Nick a child before Taylor could and went to the hospital to have her eggs checked for viability . Her eggs were mistakenly transplanted into Taylor . Both Taylor and Brooke agreed to take care of Jack . He was named after Jackie Marone , Nick 's mother , and Jack Hamilton , Taylor 's father .
