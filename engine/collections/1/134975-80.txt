The ship was to be purchased in the United States , so Garibaldi went to New York , arriving on 30 July 1850 ; however , the funds for purchasing of a ship were lacking . While in New York he stayed with various Italian friends , including some exiled revolutionaries and he attended the masonic lodges of New York in 1850 where he met several supporters of democratic internationalism , whose minds were open to making socialist thoughts their own and giving Freemasonry a strong anti-papal stance .
