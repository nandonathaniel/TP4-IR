Diet Mountain Dew Ultra Violet 2009 , 2011 A lavender - colored , mixed berry - flavored Mountain Dew . It was originally available for three months in 2009 ; and was the first Mountain Dew flavor available exclusively in Diet . It was released on 3 August 2009 at a first taste party in Brooklyn , New York . This flavor returned for the `` FanDEWmonium '' promotion , but ultimately lost to Diet Mountain Dew Supernova , coming in sixth place and not making it into the final round . Its flavor is sometimes compared to that of `` Revolution '' due to their berry - themed flavoring .
