The trade goods included gold , salt , slaves , kola nuts , leather , dates , and ivory . And by the 10th century , the Songhai chiefs had established Gao as a small kingdom , taking control of the people living along the trade routes . At around 1300 , Gao had become so prosperous that it attracted the attention of the Mali Empire and its rulers . Gao was subsequently conquered by them and Mali profited from Gao 's trade and collected taxes from its kings until about the 1430s . Troubles in the Mali homelands made it impossible to maintain control of Gao . Ibn Battuta visited Gao in 1353 when the town was a part of the Mali Empire .
