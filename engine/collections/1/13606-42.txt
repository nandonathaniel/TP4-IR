The Maryland Transit Administration canceled all service for October 29 and October 30 . The cancellations applied to buses , light rail , and Amtrak and MARC train service . On October 29 , six shelters opened in Baltimore , and early voting was canceled for the day . Maryland Insurance Commissioner Therese M. Goldsmith activated an emergency regulation requiring pharmacies to refill prescriptions regardless of their last refill date . On October 29 , the Chesapeake Bay Bridge over the Chesapeake Bay and the Millard E. Tydings Memorial Bridge and Thomas J. Hatem Memorial Bridge over the Susquehanna River were closed to traffic in the midday hours .
