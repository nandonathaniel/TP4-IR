The Balkan Peninsula is surrounded by the Adriatic Sea to the west , the Mediterranean Sea ( including the Ionian and Aegean seas ) and the Marmara Sea to the south and the Black Sea to the east . Its northern boundary is often given as the Danube , Sava and Kupa Rivers . The Balkan Peninsula has a combined area of about 470,000 km ( 181,000 sq mi ) ( slightly smaller than Spain ) . It is more or less identical to the region known as Southeastern Europe .
