In mythology , dogs often serve as pets or as watchdogs . Stories of dogs guarding the gates of the underworld recur throughout Indo - European mythologies and may originate from Proto - Indo - European religion . In Greek mythology , Cerberus is a three - headed watchdog who guards the gates of Hades . In Norse mythology , a bloody , four - eyed dog called Garmr guards Helheim .
