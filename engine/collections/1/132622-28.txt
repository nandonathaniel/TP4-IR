The photograph is of a Space Command 600 , and was the remote control designed for use with their color television receivers . The Space Command 600 was introduced in 1965 and this particular design was in use until the end of the 1972 model year . The Space Command 600 remote control had an additional , distinctive feature -- this remote control could also adjust color hues . By pressing the mute button on the remote , a relay would be activated at the television in which to transfer the VHF motor drive tuner circuit to the motorized hue control . This would allow the user to adjust the hue in increments by depressing the channel up or down buttons on the remote control , and restore the television to normal tuning operation when the mute button was pressed again ( mute off ) .
