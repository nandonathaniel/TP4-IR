For the second season , the first opening theme is `` Ignite '' by Aoi , and the first ending theme is `` Startear '' by Haruna . The second opening theme is `` Courage '' by Tomatsu , and the second ending theme is `` No More Time Machine '' by LiSA , with the third ending theme being `` Shirushi '' ( シルシ ) , also by LiSA . The song `` Catch the Moment '' by LiSA is used as the theme song to Sword Art Online : Ordinal Scale .
