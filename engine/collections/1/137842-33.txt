Wataru Takagi ( 高木 渉 , Takagi Wataru ) , Harry Wilder as dubbed by Funimation , is an officer who works for Meguire . He has feelings for his fellow officer Miwako Sato , and gets jealous whenever he hears she is interested in someone . He sometimes deflects any praises and motivational compliments she might give . Though he has an appearance of ineffectual nervousness , he is quick to assess a situation and take immediate and decisive action when necessary , and has a knack for getting out of situations out of sheer luck . His Japanese voice actor is of the same name , while his English voice actor is Doug Burks .
