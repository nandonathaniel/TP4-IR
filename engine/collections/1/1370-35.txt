Despite having the highest career batting average for an Indian test cricketer of 54 he played his last Test when he was just 23 years . He was not even considered for Test cricket and only considered for one day cricket . Currently he appears as a cricket expert and commentator on various television channels . He has also appeared on various reality shows and done few serials and Bollywood films as an actor . He played a supporting role in the Kannada film Bettanagere .
