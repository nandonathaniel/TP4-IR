It honors the willingness of Abraham ( Ibrahim ) to sacrifice his young first - born son Ishmael ( Ismail ) as an act of submission to God 's command , before God then intervened to provide Abraham with a lamb to sacrifice instead . In the lunar - based Islamic calendar , Eid al - Adha falls on the 10th day of Dhu al - Hijjah and lasts for four days . In the international Gregorian calendar , the dates vary from year to year , drifting approximately 11 days earlier each year .
