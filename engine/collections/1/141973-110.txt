8 Ramaswamy Venkataraman ( 1910 -- 2009 ) 25 July 1987 25 July 1992 60 Shankar Dayal Sharma Indian National Congress In 1942 , Venkataraman was jailed by the British for his involvement in the Indian independence movement . After his release , he was elected to independent India 's Provisional Parliament as a member of the Congress Party in 1950 and eventually joined the central government , where he first served as Minister of Finance and Industry and later as Minister of Defence .
