Suzu Kousaka ( 高坂 スズ , Kousaka Shizue ) Voiced by : Eri Nakao ( Japanese ) ; Meg McDonald ( English ) Suzu is Junpei 's younger sister and is in her first year of middle school . She pokes fun at Junpei when he is with girls and gossips with her mother about the girls Junpei is with . She is a great cat lover and loves Nyamsus very much . It is shown that she is dating Sumiyoshi 's younger brother and is not embarrassed about the relationship .
