The factory is also manufacturing 120 Linke - Hofmann - Busch ( LHB ) coaches to be exported to Bangladesh at an estimated cost of Rs 367 crore , with the first consignment of 40 set to be dispatched in March 2016 . The factory has already exported coaches to South East Asian and African countries which have Metre Gauge rail networks and Indian Railways ' past experience in Meter Gauge rolling stock has proves handy in serving these markets .
