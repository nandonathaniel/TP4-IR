Gilgit - Baltistan is administratively divided into three divisions which , in turn , are divided into ten districts , consisting of the four Baltistan districts of Skardu , Shigar , Kharmang , and Ghanche , and the four Gilgit districts of Gilgit , Ghizer , Hunza and Nagar and two districts of Diamer and Astore are part of Diamer Division . The principal administrative centers are the towns of Gilgit and Skardu .
