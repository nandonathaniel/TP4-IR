However , in implementing this plan , the Gallispan generals at the front were hampered by the orders of their respective governments . For example , the commander of the Spanish army in the field , the Prince of Conti , could not get along with , or even reason with , the Marquis de La Mina , the Supreme commander of all Spanish forces . The Prince of Conti felt that the Marquis `` deferred blindly to all orders coming from Spain '' without any consideration of the realities on the ground .
