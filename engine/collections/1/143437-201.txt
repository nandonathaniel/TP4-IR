Players born in U.S. possessions such as Puerto Rico and the U.S. , Virgin Islands , most notably USVI native Tim Duncan , are counted as `` international '' even though they are U.S. citizens by birth , and may even have represented the U.S. in international competition ( like Duncan ) . U.S. - born players are not counted as `` international '' even if they were born with citizenship in another country and represent that country internationally , such as Joakim Noah , and Kosta Koufos .
