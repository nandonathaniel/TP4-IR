POWERGRID operates throughout India . Its transmission network consists of roughly 141,920 circuit kilometers and 224 EHVAC and HVDC substations , which provide total transformation capacity of 295,980 MVA . POWERGRID 's interregional capacity is 63,650 MW . Examples of POWERGRID - owned stations include the Vizag back - to - back HVDC converter station , the Chandrapur back - to - back HVDC converter station , the India Sri Lanka HVDC Interconnection , and the Talcher -- Kolar HVDC system .
