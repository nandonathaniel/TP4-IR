Kenny and Catlett were the first cast members to receive award nominations for their performance on SpongeBob SquarePants . They each received Annie Award nominations in 2001 , but did not win . In 2010 , Kenny won the Annie Award for Best Voice Acting in a Television Production , making it the first time a cast member had won this type of award . Bumpass was the first cast member to be nominated for an Emmy Award , receiving a nomination in 2012 but losing to June Foray .
