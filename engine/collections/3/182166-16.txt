Healey began playing guitar when he was three , developing his unique style of playing the instrument flat on his lap . At age 9 his musical talents were showcased in an interview on the TVOntario children 's programme , The Cucumber Show . When he was 15 , Jeff Healey formed the band Blue Direction , a four - piece which primarily played bar - band cover tunes and featured bassist Jeremy Littler , drummer Graydon Chapman , and a schoolmate , Rob Quail on second guitar . This band played various local clubs in Toronto , including the Colonial Tavern .
