América is one of the most successful and most decorated clubs in Mexican football history . The club has won 16 league titles ( 4 amateur , 12 professional ) , 6 Copa México championships ( 1 amateur , 5 professional ) , 5 Campeón de Campeones , 1 Copa Challenger and 1 InterLiga tournament . In international competitions , América has won 10 titles , the most for a club from the CONCACAF region . The club has won a record 7 CONCACAF Champions ' Cup / Champions League titles , 2 Copa Interamericana cups , and 1 CONCACAF Giants Cup .
