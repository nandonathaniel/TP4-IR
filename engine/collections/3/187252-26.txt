Meanwhile , Tashtego informs Tubbs that he has put together a small team to hunt down the Creeper and kill it once and for all . Later , a group of teenagers discover the Creeper 's truck in a field . After finding the bodies , they take off on dirt bikes , but Kirk is shot in the leg with the spear . As two of the other boys try to free him , the Creeper returns and kills them both with another spear . The last boy is abducted by the Creeper as he tries to escape after peeing on the Creeper 's truck .
