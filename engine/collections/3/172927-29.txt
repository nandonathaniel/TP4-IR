`` Setting the World on Fire '' is a song written by Ross Copperman , Matt Jenkins , and Josh Osborne and recorded by American country music artist Kenny Chesney , featuring American singer Pink . It was released to country radio on July 28 , 2016 and to digital retailers the following day as the second single from Chesney 's seventeenth studio album , Cosmic Hallelujah . The song was also promoted to adult and pop radio on September 12 , 2016 . The song was nominated for the Best Country Duo / Group Performance at the 2017 Grammy Awards .
