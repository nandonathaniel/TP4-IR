An EggRobo ( エッグロボ , Eggurobo ) is an egg - shaped humanoid robot which resembles Doctor Eggman . They first appeared as enemies in the game Sonic & Knuckles . In Sonic 's storyline , they only appeared as standard enemies in the Sky Sanctuary Zone . In Knuckles ' story , however , one EggRobo in particular replaces Eggman as the end - of - zone boss in a number of levels . An EggRobo later appears in Sonic R as a playable character , and in the form of a kart racer in Sonic Adventure 2 .
