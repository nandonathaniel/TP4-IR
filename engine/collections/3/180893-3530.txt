Mexican -- American War . 3rd Tennessee Infantry : colonel , May 3 , 1861 . Captured at Vicksburg , July 4 , 1863 . Exchanged October 13 , 1863 . Commanded cavalry brigade during Shenandoah Valley Campaign ( 1864 ) . With Jubal Early in march on Washington . Wounded twice at Martinsburg , West Virginia . Brigade formed part of Jefferson Davis 's escort in flight south after fall of Richmond . Paroled May 9 , 1865 , Washington , Georgia .
