Bing Crosby recorded the story on April 25 , 1957 , linking the narrative with songs . This was issued as an album Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves in 1957 . The Beastie Boys reference Ali Baba in their song `` Rhymin ' and Stealin ' '' ( 1986 ) . The second track on `` super group '' Dark Lotus ' album Tales from the Lotus Pod ( 2001 ) is titled `` Ali Baba '' . John Holt sings of the dream he had of Ali Baba in his song titled `` Ali Baba '' .
