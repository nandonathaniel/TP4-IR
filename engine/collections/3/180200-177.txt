Even though it won the conflict , the war had a profound and devastating long - term effect in the Soviet Union . The financial burden was catastrophic : by one estimate , the Soviet Union spent $192 billion . The US lend - lease around $11 billion in supplies to the Soviet Union during the war . Anastasia V. Zotova gives a slightly different estimate of 666.4 billion rubles in Soviet military expenditure during the war , equivalent to $125.7 billion .
