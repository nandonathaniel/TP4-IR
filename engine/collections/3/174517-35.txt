Karen Malina White : Dijonay Jones , Penny 's selfish , untrustworthy , at times caring , and enthusiastic best friend . She has an obsessive crush on Sticky . She also loves to gossip and has gotten Penny in trouble many times . Eventually , Penny had to stop trusting Dijonay , though they still remain friends . Dijonay 's name is a pun on the name of Best Foods ' / Hellmann 's Djionnaise : and she has 9 younger siblings all named after spices , seasonings and condiments .
