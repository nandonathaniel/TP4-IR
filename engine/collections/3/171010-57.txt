He plays domestic cricket for Jamaica , and also represents the Kings XI Punjab in the Indian Premier League , the Karachi Kings in the Pakistan Super League , the Melbourne Renegades in the Big Bash League and the Rangpur Riders in the Bangladesh Premier League . He has also represented Worcestershire , the Western Warriors , Sydney Thunder , Barisal Burners , Kolkata Knight Riders and Somerset in his career . He was also selected for team Uva Next for the inaugural Sri Lanka Premier League in 2012 . In 2017 -- 18 Bangladesh Premier League season , Gayle performed for Rangpur Riders and he came out as the player of the series as well as the highest run scorer .
