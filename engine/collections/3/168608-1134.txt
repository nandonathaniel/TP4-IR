Su Wu ( voiced by Sumalee Montano in Kung Fu Panda : Secrets of the Masters , Susan Blakeslee in the Kung Fu Panda video game , Jennifer Darling in Kung Fu Panda : Legendary Warriors ) - The eldest of the Wu Sisters who is the most ambitious and ruthless of the group . She is distinguishable from her sisters by her blind left eye which is blue while her right eye is gold .
