January 17 , 1969 Los Angeles , California 0 Alprentice Carter and John Huggins , two student members of the Black Panther Party , were fatally shot during a student meeting inside Campbell Hall at the University of California , Los Angeles . Reportedly there was disagreement over who would control the school 's African American Studies Center . The shooter , Claude Hubert , was never found ; three other men were later arrested in connection with the shooting .
