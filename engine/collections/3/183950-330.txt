This was Buck 's seventh and last World Series call for CBS Radio , as he moved to CBS ' television coverage of baseball the following year . Bench continued to call the World Series on radio through 1993 as Vin Scully 's color man . Locally , the 1989 World Series was broadcast by KSFO - AM in San Francisco with Bill King , Lon Simmons and Ray Fosse , and by KNBR - AM in San Francisco with Hank Greenwald and Ron Fairly .
