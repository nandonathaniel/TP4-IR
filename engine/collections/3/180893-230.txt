Bee , Barnard E. Jr . Colonel Brigadier General rank : June 17 , 1861 nom : June 17 , 1861 conf : August 28 , 1861 ( posthumous ) USMA , 1845 . Wounded in Mexican -- American War . Brother of Hamilton Bee . Resigned as captain , U.S. Army , March 3 , 1861 . 1st South Carolina Regulars , artillery regiment , lieutenant colonel , June 1 , 1861 . Posthumous confirmation of brigadier general promotion over a month after death . Eicher , Warner list as a general . Gave `` Stonewall '' Jackson his famous nickname at First Bull Run , where Bee was mortally wounded July 21 , 1861 , and died July 22 , 1861 .
