Pokémon Yellow : Special Pikachu Edition ( ポケットモンスター ピカチュウ , Poketto Monsutā Pikachū , lit . `` Pocket Monsters Pikachu '' ) was a special edition of the Red and Blue versions , and was originally released on September 12 , 1998 , in Japan , with releases in North America and Europe on October 1 , 1999 , and June 16 , 2000 , respectively . The game was designed to resemble the Pokémon anime series , with the player receiving a Pikachu as his starter Pokémon , and his rival starting with an Eevee . Some non-player characters resemble those from the anime , including Team Rocket : Jessie and James .
