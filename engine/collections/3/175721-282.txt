Decasyllable ( decassílabo ) : composed of 10 syllables . Mostly used in Parnassian sonnets . It is equivalent to the Italian hendecasyllable . Heroic ( heróico ) : stresses on the sixth and tenth syllables . Sapphic ( sáfico ) : stresses on the fourth , eighth and tenth syllables . Martelo : stresses on the third , sixth and tenth syllables . Gaita galega or moinheira : stresses on the fourth , seventh and tenth syllables .
