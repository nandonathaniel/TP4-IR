In 2013 , businessman Jackie Robinson , a former UNLV student and NBA player , announced that he was planning on using the same site of the proposed Silver State Arena to build the All Net Resort and Arena , a $1.4 billion privately funded complex encompassing an arena , hotel and shopping project that could attract an NBA franchise to Las Vegas . The arena itself would cost $670 million . The project is delayed until 2018 or 2019 .
