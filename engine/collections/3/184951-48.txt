For example , Chesapeake is divided into six boroughs , one corresponding to the former city of South Norfolk and one corresponding to each of the five magisterial districts of the former Norfolk County . Suffolk is divided into seven boroughs , one corresponding to the former city of Suffolk and one corresponding to each of the six magisterial districts of the former Nansemond County . In Virginia Beach , the seven boroughs were abolished effective July 1 , 1998 .
