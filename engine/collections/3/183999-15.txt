In 2014 , Dhawan had two film releases . His first appearance was in the comedy Main Tera Hero ( 2014 ) , a remake of the Telugu film Kandireega , which was produced by Balaji Motion Pictures and directed by his father . He played the leading role of Sreenath `` Seenu '' Prasad , an impetuous brat , who is in love with Ileana D'Cruz 's character but is seduced by the character played by Nargis Fakhri . Raedita Tandon of Filmfare commended Dhawan on his comic timing and compared him favorably to Govinda and Prabhudheva .
