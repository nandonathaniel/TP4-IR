Save Ganga Movement is a widespread Gandhian non-violent movement supported by saints and popular social activists across the Indian States Uttar Pradesh and Bihar in support of a free Ganga . The movement is supported by Ganga Seva Abhiyanam , Pune - based National Women 's Organisation ( ndO ) besides those of many other like - minded organisations and with the moral support from many religious leaders , spiritual and political , scientists , environmentalists , writers and social activists . Ganga Calling -- Save Ganga is another such campaign supported by Indian Council for Enviro - Legal Action ( ICELA ) .
