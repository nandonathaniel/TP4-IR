In the face of massive Soviet bewilderment , the Molotov -- Ribbentrop Pact was defended by speaker in Gorky Park . Molotov defended it in an article in Pravda proclaiming that it was a treaty between states , not systems . Stalin himself devised diagrams to show that Chamberlain had wanted to pit the USSR against Nazi Germany , but Comrade Stalin had wisely pit Great Britain against Nazi Germany .
