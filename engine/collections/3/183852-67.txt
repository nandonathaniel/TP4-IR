This title was added as part of the 1990 amendments . It addresses the issue of acid rain , which is caused by nitrogen oxides ( NO ) and sulfur dioxide ( SO ) emissions from electric power plants powered by fossil fuels , and other industrial sources . The 1990 amendments gave industries more pollution control options including switching to low - sulfur coal and / or adding devices that controlled the harmful emissions . In some cases plants had to be closed down to prevent the dangerous chemicals from entering the atmosphere .
