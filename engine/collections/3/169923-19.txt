Harley Quinn ( full name : Dr. Harleen Frances Quinzel ) is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics . The character was created by Paul Dini and Bruce Timm , and first appeared in Batman : The Animated Series in September 1992 . She later appeared in DC Comics 's Batman comic books , with the character 's first comic book appearance in The Batman Adventures # 12 ( September 1993 ) .
