The next morning , the boys arrive on time to meet Payne . However , Payne does n't sugarcoat them , warning them they will be doing some extra hard work on their path to becoming musical artists . Payne comes up with the group 's name of New Edition , as if they were either a new version or new edition of the Jacksons . Weeks of strenuous training features time spent on choreography , physical and mental discipline , and singing , Payne prepares the quartet in for a big talent showcase at the Strand Theater , where the winning act will receive a recording contract from Maurice Starr ( Faizon Love ) under his independent record label .
