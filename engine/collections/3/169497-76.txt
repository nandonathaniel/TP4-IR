Another composer Armando Manzanero widely considered the premier Mexican romantic composer of the postwar era and one of the most successful composers of Latin America has composed more than four hundred songs , fifty of which have given him international fame . His most famous songs include Voy a apagar la luz ( I 'm Going to Turn Off the Lights ) , Contigo Aprendí ( With you I Learnt ... ) , Adoro ( Adore ) , No sé tú ( I do n't know if you ... ) , Por Debajo de la Mesa ( Under the Table ) Esta Tarde Vi Llover ( English version `` Yesterday I Heard the Rain '' ) , Somos Novios ( English version `` It 's Impossible '' ) , Felicidad ( Happiness ) and Nada Personal ( Nothing Personal ) .
