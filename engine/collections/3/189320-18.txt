An infant who acts in an adult way , Stewie was initially obsessed with violence and matricide . He is the youngest child of Peter and Lois Griffin , and the youngest brother of Meg and Chris . Over the duration of the series , the violent aspects of Stewie 's personality have been toned down , and he has evolved into an eccentric , friendly and flamboyant character . He has also come to have a very close friendship with the family 's anthropomorphic dog , Brian . Stewie is considered to be the show 's breakout character . Wizard magazine rated him the 95th greatest villain of all time .
