In 1982 , Rhino Records released a compilation of Beatles - related novelty and parody songs , called Beatlesongs . It featured a cover caricature of Chapman by William Stout . Following its release , Rhino recalled the record and replaced it with another cover . New York - based band Mindless Self Indulgence released a track entitled `` Mark David Chapman '' on their album If . Irish band the Cranberries recorded a song called `` I Just Shot John Lennon '' for their 1996 album To the Faithful Departed , citing events that took place outside the Dakota on the night of Lennon 's murder . The title of the song comes from Chapman 's own words .
