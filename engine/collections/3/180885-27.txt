Nicholas `` Nick '' Jonathan Barkley , the hot - tempered younger son , was portrayed by Peter Breck . Nick managed the family ranch . He wore a black leather vest , large black hat and black leather gloves , and was distinguished by his brawling ways and loud demeanor . Nick had served as a soldier in combat in the Union Army during the Civil War as referenced in the episode `` Forty Rifles . '' He was always ready for a fight , and at times would fight with his brothers as well . Underneath his gruff exterior , he was fun - loving , had a great sense of humor , was warm , caring and loved his family deeply . Breck appeared in 101 of the 112 episodes .
