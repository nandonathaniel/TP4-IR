When a female escort ( Morgan Taylor Campbell ) is found sexually assaulted and brutally beaten , the Special Victims Unit are called in to investigate . They soon discover that she was in a sleazy hotel with three soldiers from the military . However , the case becomes complicated when one soldier ( Marquise Vilson ) refuses to talk and another ( Jack DiFalco ) confesses to the crime , even though it is clear that he is lying . Eventually , when the real rapist is found and the DNA comes in , the case takes an extremely shocking twist and a huge sacrifice is made by one of the soldiers who has been hiding a secret from the world for a very long time . Meanwhile , Rollins ' unprofessional work in the case and taking it personally leads to her revealing a secret from her recent romantic life .
