Hamilton arrived at the Spanish Grand Prix with three consecutive victories behind him and the opportunity to move ahead of Rosberg in the championship with win number four . Hamilton held off a charging Rosberg late in the race , crossing the line just 0.6 s ahead of the German . On a track with more overtaking opportunities , Rosberg may have been able to pass and after the race said an extra lap would have been enough . It was revealed after the race that Hamilton had used the same engine mode to defend position that Rosberg had used in Bahrain .
