In addition to flights to its main regional hub at Charlotte Douglas International Airport , US Airways introduced three daily flights between Wilmington and La Guardia Airport in New York City during the 2000s following lobbying from the Wilmington community ; however , following US Airways ' planned transfer of La Guardia slots to Delta Air Lines , there is speculation that these flights will cease . US Airways also introduced nonstop service to Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport in March 2011 .
