February 4 , 2015 Frederick , Maryland 0 Two students were shot near the gymnasium of Frederick High School during a junior varsity ( JV ) boys ' basketball game . Approximately two hundred students , staff , and faculty were placed on lockdown for several hours after the shooting while police searched for the suspects . No suspects have been apprehended , although witnesses report seeing four black males dressed all in black who are considered by police to be suspects in this shooting .
