With a long history of literature in the form of Middle Persian before Islam , Persian was the first language in the Muslim world to break through Arabic 's monopoly on writing , and the writing of poetry in Persian was established as a court tradition in many eastern courts . Some of the famous works of Persian literature are the Shahnameh of Ferdowsi , the works of Rumi , the Rubaiyat of Omar Khayyam , the Panj Ganj of Nizami Ganjavi , the DivÄn of Hafez and the two miscellanea of prose and verse by Saadi Shirazi , the Gulistan and the Bustan .
