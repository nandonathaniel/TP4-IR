Tinnitus Tinnitus often results in the sound of ringing . Pronunciation / ˈtɪnɪtəs / or / tɪˈnaɪtəs / Specialty Otorhinolaryngology , audiology Symptoms Hearing sound when no external sound is present Complications Depression , anxiety , poor concentration Usual onset Gradual Causes Noise - induced hearing loss , ear infections , disease of the heart or blood vessels , Ménière 's disease , brain tumors , emotional stress Diagnostic method Based on symptoms , audiogram , neurological exam Treatment Counseling , sound generators , hearing aids Frequency ~ 12.5 %
