Vicki and the Solomons continue to search for Harry , who was abducted and sold to a circus by Vicki 's ex , Randy , where Harry 's transmissions from The Big Giant Head award him popularity as the act `` Hargo the Alien '' . Meanwhile , the loss of Harry means that the aliens ' status report continues to remain past - due , for which The Big Giant Head 's punishment is to slowly cease Dick 's brain functions . The group races to find and convince Harry to transmit their report and come home to the family and Rutherford before it is too late for Dick . Phil Hartman , who appeared as Randy in `` Eat , Drink , Mary , '' part 1 of this storyline and the season 3 finale , was shot and killed by his wife 8 days after the episode aired . His character does not appear in this episode .
