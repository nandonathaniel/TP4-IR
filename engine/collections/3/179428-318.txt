Poland played at the 2018 FIFA World Cup , their first World Cup since 2006 , in Group H ; against Senegal , Colombia and Japan . Despite the group being considered a close group , Poland were tipped as favorites to advance from the group . In May 2018 , Poland named the preliminary 34 - man squad , and on 4 June , they named the final 23 - man squad . The squad featured several notable players ; such as elite striker Robert Lewandowski ( playing at his first World Cup ) , VfL Wolfsburg 's veteran midfielder Jakub Błaszczykowski , Monaco defender Kamil Glik ( who suffered an injury two weeks prior to the start of the tournament ) , Napoli forward Arkadiusz Milik and Juventus goalkeeper Wojciech Szczęsny .
