There are over twice as many gun - related suicides than gun - related homicides in the United States . Firearms are the most popular method of suicide due to the lethality of the weapon . 90 % of all suicides attempted using a firearm result in a fatality , as opposed to less than 3 % of suicide attempts involving cutting or drug - use . The risk of someone attempting suicide is also 4.8 times greater if they are exposed to a firearm on a regular basis ; for example , in the home .
