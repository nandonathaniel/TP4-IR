Sim Aloo ( `` Imperial Dignitary '' ) Anthony Lang ( Return of the Jedi ) Member of the Imperial Ruling Council and one of Emperor Palpatine 's advisors , he appears alongside other councilors to the Emperor on the second Death Star , and is killed when it is destroyed over the forest moon of Endor in Return of the Jedi . An action figure of this character , entitled `` Imperial Dignitary '' , was created in Kenner 's 1985 Power of the Force line .
