They have the radio callsigns Japanese Air Force One and Japanese Air Force Two when operating on official business , and Cygnus One and Cygnus Two when operating outside of official business ( e.g. , on training flights ). ( 2 ) The aircraft always fly together on government missions , with one serving as the primary transport and the other serving as a backup with maintenance personnel on board . The aircraft are officially referred to as Japanese government exclusive aircraft ( 日本 国 政府 専用 機 Nippon - koku seifu sen'yōki ) .
