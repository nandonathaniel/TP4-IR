Harris made his directorial debut in 2000 with the drama biopic Pollock , in which he also starred as artist Jackson Pollock . He was nominated for his first Academy Award for Best Actor ( and third Oscar overall ) for his performance . To prepare for the role , he built a small studio in which to copy the painter 's techniques . Two years later , Harris was nominated for his fourth Academy Award ( third in the Best Supporting Actor category ) for his role as Richard Brown in the British - American drama film The Hours .
