Won at least one World Golf Championships event every year from 1999 to 2009 . All - time wins leader in World Golf Championships events ( 41.9 % win rate ) . All - time money leader in World Golf Championships events . Won the WGC - World Cup with David Duval in 2000 , an unofficial money WGC event , for a total of 19 World Golf Championships titles . Only player to have held the titles of all three of the pre-2009 events ( not counting the HSBC Champions , elevated to WGC status in 2009 ) at the same time .
