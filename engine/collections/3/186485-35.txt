But Parry began to have misgivings again about Fight for Right and eventually wrote to Sir Francis Younghusband withdrawing his support entirely in May 1917 . There was even concern that the composer might withdraw the song , but the situation was saved by Millicent Fawcett of the National Union of Women 's Suffrage Societies ( NUWSS ) . The song had been taken up by the Suffragettes in 1917 and Fawcett asked Parry if it might be used at a Suffrage Demonstration Concert on 13 March 1918 . Parry was delighted and orchestrated the piece for the concert ( it had originally been for voices and organ ) .
