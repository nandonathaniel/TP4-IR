The music video for `` Run '' was filmed in 2003 , in a remote area of the county of Kent . It was directed by Paul Gore , produced by Suzie Morton through the company Flynn Productions , photographed by Ben Smithard , and edited by Tony Kearns , and it premiered in October 2003 . During filming , people from the area called the police , because they noticed several red distress flares were used at night , which were those Snow Patrol members used .
