In 2009 , the state also purchased 1,174 acres ( 4.75 km ) in Jefferson Township the former site of the Mount Paul monastery and seminary belonging to Paulist Fathers ( from 1924 -- 2009 ) . The tract , which will be developed into a state park , is located in the state 's Highlands region on the eastern side of Sparta Mountain and featuring mountain streams that flow into the Russia Brook ( a tributary of the Rockaway River ) .
