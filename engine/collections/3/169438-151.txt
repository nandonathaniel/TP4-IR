On May 25 , the Giants held a celebration for Baseball Hall of Famer Juan Marichal . A statue of Marichál was dedicated on the plaza outside the stadium . Leonel Fernández , the President of the Dominican Republic , attended . In the two games following the ceremonies , the Giants wore uniforms with the word GIGANTES ( Spanish for `` Giants '' ) on the front of their jerseys . On July 14 , 2005 , the franchise won its 10,000 th game , defeating their longtime rival Dodgers , 4 -- 3 and thereby becoming the first professional sports franchise to have a five - figure win total .
