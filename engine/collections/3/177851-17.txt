The Portuguese Atlantic island possession of the Azores is 1,368 km ( 850 mi ) from Europe and 1,507 km ( 936 mi ) from Africa , and is usually grouped with Europe if grouped with any continent . By contrast , the Canary and Madeira islands off the Atlantic coast of Morocco are much closer to , and usually grouped with , Africa ( the Canary Islands are only 100 km ( 62 mi ) from the African coast at their closest point , while Madeira is 520 km ( 320 mi ) from Africa and 1,000 km ( 620 mi ) from Europe ) .
